## Why Goblin Giant Sparky Is the Ultimate Zap Bait

![Goblin Giant Sparky deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Goblin Giant costs 6 elixir. He has 3,200 HP, carries 2 Spear Goblins on his back (108 DPS combined), and drops 2 Spear Goblins on death. He is the perfect Sparky tank: high HP, constant chip damage, and walking Spear Goblins that clear swarms.

Sparky costs 6 elixir. She deals 1,100 area damage per shot with a 4-second charge. Behind Goblin Giant, she is protected from ground troops AND the Spear Goblins on GG's back clear small swarms before they reach her.

Total push: 12 elixir (GG 6 + Sparky 6). Opponent must either reset Sparky (Zap, E-Wiz) or kill Goblin Giant before Sparky fires. Most cannot do both.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026): GG Sparky has a 54% win rate at 4,000-6,000 trophies. Against decks without reset cards: 68% win rate. Against decks with 2+ reset cards: 42%. The matchup is entirely reset-dependent.

## The Zap Bait Sequence

Your opponent has 1-2 reset cards (Zap, Electro Wizard, Zappies). Your job: force them to use their reset BEFORE Sparky fires.

**Bait sequence:**
1. Dark Prince charging at bridge (4 elixir). He demands a response. They Zap to cancel charge. Zap down.
2. Goblin Giant at back. Sparky behind. Walk to bridge.
3. As they approach: Electro Wizard at bridge opposite lane (4 elixir). They must use their second reset (Zappies, second Zap) or E-Wiz connects.
4. Their resets are now ALL on cooldown. GG + Sparky reach the tower.
5. Sparky fires. 1,100 area damage. Tower + defending troops destroyed.

This sequence burns 2 reset cards before Sparky enters their range. By the time she fires, they have nothing.

## Goblin Giant: More Than a Tank

Goblin Giant's 2 Spear Goblins on his back deal 108 DPS combined. Against Skeleton Army placed on GG: Spear Goblins kill 3 skeletons per second. Skeleton Army (15) is cleared in 5 seconds. Without Spear Goblins, Skeleton Army surrounds GG and Sparky and kills both.

GG's death spawn (2 Spear Goblins) provides chip damage after he dies. They target the tower: 108 DPS. Over 5 seconds: 540 tower damage. The opponent killed the tank but gave you free chip.

## Sparky Protection Layers

Sparky has 1,200 HP. She dies to: Rocket (1,232 — one-shot), Lightning (996 — leaves 204 HP), Fireball + Zap (497 — leaves 703 HP). Protection layers:

1. **Goblin Giant in front:** Blocks ground troops and most ranged attacks
2. **Dark Prince beside Sparky:** Shield eats first spell or troop hit
3. **Electro Wizard behind:** Stuns any troop that gets past GG
4. **Zap ready:** If they Zap Sparky, you Zap their E-Wiz/Zappies in response

With 4 layers, Sparky fires in 80% of pushes.

## When to Play GG + Sparky

**Safe timing:**
- After they have used their reset card (Zap, E-Wiz)
- When they have 5 or fewer elixir
- In double elixir (faster cycle, more support)

**Unsafe timing:**
- When they have 10 elixir AND a reset card in cycle
- When they have Rocket (one-shots Sparky for +0 trade)
- When they just played P.E.K.K.A (kills GG then Sparky)
