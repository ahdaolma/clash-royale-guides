## Arena 8 (Frozen Peak): Control Decks Are Born

Arena 8 (Frozen Peak, 2300-2600 trophies) introduces the Control archetype to Clash Royale. You unlock **Ice Wizard (3), Lumberjack (4), Bowler (5), Freeze (4), Ice Golem (2), Cannon Cart (5), Giant Snowball (2), and Clone (3).**

Ice Wizard is the game's premier defensive card — 3 elixir for a 35% slow that turns any push into a crawl. Lumberjack drops Rage on death (35% speed boost for 7.5 seconds), enabling the LumberLoon combo that has been tournament-viable since 2017. Bowler's rolling boulder knocks back and damages everything in a line — the ultimate ground-defense card.

RoyaleAPI data shows that Frozen Peak is where deck archetypes crystallize: 62% of players here have settled into either Beatdown, Control, or Siege, compared to 38% in Royal Arena.

## Deck #1: LumberLoon Freeze (61% Win Rate)

![LumberLoon Freeze deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.abc123&f=1)

LumberLoon is the most iconic combo introduced by Arena 8. The sequence: play Lumberjack at the bridge, then Balloon directly behind him. Lumberjack tanks tower shots while Balloon flies toward the tower. When Lumberjack dies (usually at the tower), he drops Rage — Balloon now attacks 35% faster. A Raged Balloon at level 6 deals 1,320 damage per bomb hit. Two bombs = 2,640 damage, more than a tower's 2,534 HP.

Add Freeze to this, and the tower dies every single time the combo connects.

|------|--------|------|
| Lumberjack | 4 | Tank for Balloon / drops Rage |
| Balloon | 5 | Win condition |
| Freeze | 4 | Locks tower + defense |
| Ice Wizard | 3 | Defense / slow support |
| Bowler | 5 | Ground defense |
| Mega Minion | 3 | Air DPS |
| Zap | 2 | Reset / swarm |
| Ice Golem | 2 | Kite / mini-tank |

**Average elixir: 3.5**

### LumberLoon Combo Timing

The combo costs 9 elixir (Lumberjack 4 + Balloon 5). Always play it from 10 elixir. Sequence:
1. Lumberjack at the bridge (10 → 6 elixir)
2. Wait 0.5 seconds — let Lumberjack walk 2 tiles forward
3. Balloon behind Lumberjack (7 → 2 elixir)
4. Lumberjack reaches tower in ~3 seconds, dies in ~2 seconds of tower fire
5. Rage drops — Balloon is now Raged
6. Freeze the tower + any defensive troops (cost: 4 more elixir, generated during the push)
7. Raged Balloon destroys tower in 2 bombs

Total elixir cycle: 13 over 10 seconds (9 initial + 4 Freeze from regen). Opponent needs 10+ elixir within a 3-second window to stop this. Most Arena 8 players fail.

### When NOT to LumberLoon

- They have Inferno Tower in cycle (Lumberjack dies before reaching tower, no Rage)
- They have Rocket (one-shots Balloon for +1 trade)
- They have both Tornado AND a building (Tornado pulls Balloon to King Tower, building tanks)
- You have less than 7 elixir (partial combo gets zero damage)

## Deck #2: Ice Wizard Tornado Control (IceBow Variant, 58% Win Rate)

Ice Wizard (3 elixir) + Tornado (3 elixir) is the most elixir-efficient defensive combo in Frozen Peak. Ice Wizard slows everything by 35%. Tornado pulls everything into a tight cluster. Combined with splash (Bowler, Baby Dragon, or Executioner), you can defend 12-elixir pushes for 8-10 elixir.

**Average elixir: 3.8** — spell-cycle heavy.

### Ice Wizard + Tornado: The Numbers

Against a Giant + Witch + Musketeer push (14 elixir):
1. Ice Wizard (3 elixir) slows the entire push by 35%
2. Bowler (5 elixir) knocks back support troops, dealing 298 per boulder
3. Tornado (3 elixir) pulls everything into Bowler's boulder path
4. Total defense cost: 11 elixir. Push denied. Bowler and Ice Wizard survive for counter-push.
5. Rocket the tower in overtime for chip (1,232 damage × 2 = 2,464 — nearly a full tower)

This defensive sequence works against EVERY ground-based push in Arena 8. It is not matchup-dependent — it is mathematically unbeatable for ground troops.

### Rocket Cycle Math

Once you establish defensive control, the gameplan shifts to Rocket cycle. In overtime:
- You generate 1 elixir per 2.8 seconds
- Rocket costs 6 elixir, deals 1,232 damage
- You can Rocket every 17 seconds (6 × 2.8)
- Two Rockets = 2,464 damage vs tower's 2,534 HP
- Third Rocket wins

Defend for 3 minutes, then Rocket 3 times. This is the IceBow win condition.

## Deck #3: Bowler Graveyard Control (57% Win Rate)

![Bowler Graveyard deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.abc123&f=1)

Bowler + Graveyard is a control archetype that works from Arena 8 through Ultimate Champion. Bowler's boulder clears ground troops trying to defend Graveyard. Graveyard spawns 16 skeletons over 10 seconds — 1,072 total damage if unopposed. Together, they force the opponent to answer two threats simultaneously.

### The Deck

| Card | Elixir | Role |
|------|--------|------|
| Bowler | 5 | Tank + ground splash |
| Graveyard | 5 | Win condition |
| Ice Wizard | 3 | Slow + defense |
| Tornado | 3 | Pull + cluster |
| Freeze | 4 | Surprise spell |
| Mega Minion | 3 | Air DPS |
| Zap | 2 | Small spell |
| Ice Golem | 2 | Kite / mini-tank |

**Average elixir: 3.4**

### Bowler + Graveyard Push

1. Bowler behind King Tower (8 elixir)
2. Bowler crosses bridge, enemy drops ground defense (Barbarians, Valkyrie, Skeleton Army)
3. Graveyard on their tower (now 4 elixir remaining)
4. Bowler's boulder rolls through their defense, clearing everything
5. Graveyard skeletons accumulate — 4-5 skeletons hitting the tower simultaneously = 268-335 DPS
6. If they play swarm defense, Zap or Ice Wizard splash clears it

The key: Bowler MUST be alive when Graveyard starts spawning. If Bowler dies, ground troops kill your skeletons. If Bowler lives, his boulder clears ground troops and skeletons have 10 seconds of free damage.

### Freeze Surprise

In double elixir, add Freeze to the push: Bowler → Graveyard → Freeze the tower. They place defense → Frozen. Tower cannot shoot → Skeletons and Bowler deal 10 seconds of uncontested damage. This one push usually takes a tower from full HP to zero.
