## Why Miner Control Is the Most Versatile Archetype

![Miner Control deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Miner costs 3 elixir. At tournament standard: 1,060 HP, 192 DPS, and can be placed ANYWHERE on the map. He is a guaranteed tower connection — no building can block him because he deploys directly on the tower. His 192 DPS seems low, but over a 3-minute match, 8-12 Miner connections deal 1,500-2,300 tower damage. Combined with Poison (810 chip per cast), Miner Control kills towers through pure chip accumulation.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026): Miner has a 52% win rate overall but a 59% win rate in matches lasting beyond 2 minutes. Miner Control gets stronger the longer the match goes — chip damage is permanent and adds up.

## The Core Combo: Miner + Poison

Miner on tower (3 elixir) + Poison on tower area (4 elixir) = 7 elixir. Miner deals 192 DPS while tanking. Poison deals 810 damage over 8 seconds and kills any swarm defense (Skeleton Army, Goblin Gang, Bats). Tower takes Miner DPS × number of seconds Miner lives + 810 Poison damage.

**Damage per push:** Miner tanks 5-6 tower shots (5-6 seconds). Miner damage: 192 × 5 = 960. Poison damage: 810. Total: 1,770 tower damage. 7 elixir for 1,770 damage. Two pushes = 3,540 — more than a tower's 2,534 HP.

## Miner Placement Mixups

The key to Miner Control is unpredictable placement. If your opponent correctly predicts Miner's location, they drop a troop and deny all damage. If they guess wrong, Miner gets free damage.

**Standard placement mixup pattern:**
1. First Miner: behind the tower (most common, most players defend here)
2. Second Miner: front of the tower (they pre-placed defense behind — Miner dodges it)
3. Third Miner: left side of tower (they now cover front AND back — Miner dodges both)
4. Fourth Miner: right side of tower
5. Repeat in random order

**Advanced pattern:** when they have a defensive troop behind the tower waiting for Miner, Miner the OPPOSITE lane. They waste their troop. Next rotation: Miner the original lane — their troop is now out of cycle.

## Poison Value: Hit Tower + Troops Simultaneously

Poison is never just for tower chip. Always aim to hit:
1. The tower (810 damage)
2. Any defensive troop they place (at least 1 troop = +value)
3. Any building (Cannon, Tesla, Bomb Tower)

If Poison hits tower + Cannon + Musketeer, it deals 810 to tower + 500 to Cannon + kills Musketeer. 4 elixir for 810 tower damage + 5 elixir of troop/building damage. Massive value.

## Defensive Core: Inferno Tower + Electro Wizard

Inferno Tower (5 elixir) at 4-3 center melts any tank. Electro Wizard (4) stuns support troops and resets Inferno Tower if it gets Zapped. Together, they stop every beatdown push in the game.

**Defense sequence:** Golem + Night Witch push. Inferno Tower locks onto Golem. E-Wiz stuns Night Witch + Bats. They Zap Inferno Tower. E-Wiz's attack continues stunning — Inferno beam ramps again. Golem dies. Night Witch dies. Surviving Inferno Tower (30% HP) + E-Wiz (80% HP) now form a counter-push. Miner tanks for them.

---

## Bandit + Royal Ghost: Bridge Pressure

Bandit (3 elixir) dashes to tower, dealing 319 dash damage. Royal Ghost (3 elixir) turns invisible and splashes defending troops. Together: 6 elixir, very fast, forces immediate response. Play them when opponent is low on elixir (just spent 5+ on a push). They may connect for 500+ tower damage + bait out their defense before your Miner+Poison cycle.

## Poison Cycle Math: Win in Overtime

In overtime (1 elixir/1.4 seconds), Poison cycles every 4 cards × 3.1 avg = 12.4 elixir = 17.4 seconds. Tower takes 810 per Poison. 2,534 ÷ 810 = 3.1 Poisons to kill tower. Four Poisons = 3,240 damage = tower dead. Time: 4 × 17.4 = 70 seconds. Defend for 60-70 seconds of overtime, Poison 4 times, win.
