## 10 Tips to Master Mortar Bait

### 1. Mortar One Tile Behind Bridge, Never At

Mortar at bridge: enemy Giant dropped on it = Mortar dead in 4 seconds. Mortar one tile back: Giant must walk around river. 2 extra seconds = 2 extra Mortar shots (440 damage). One tile = doubled Mortar value.

### 2. Skeleton Barrel + Mortar Simultaneously

Mortar at bridge → opponent drops Knight → Skeleton Barrel on tower. Knight is on Mortar, not tower. Barrel drops 8 skeletons. Tower now has Mortar shells (220 each) + 8 skeletons (536 DPS) hitting it. Tower dead in 5 seconds. Opponent needs 7+ elixir to answer both. Most have 4.

### 3. Defensive Mortar Wins Control Matchups

Against beatdown (Golem, Giant): Mortar between your towers. 220 splash per shot on support troops. Knight tanks Golem. Goblin Gang surrounds support. Defensive Mortar provides more value than offensive Mortar in these matchups. Know when to switch.

### 4. Princess Must Alternate Lanes

Princess at bridge left lane → they Log. Next Princess: right lane. They hesitate. Princess gets 1 extra shot (140 damage). Over 6 Princess plays: 3 extra shots, 420 free damage. Alternating lanes forces hesitation — hesitation = free damage.

### 5. Dart Goblin at Bridge Is a Win Condition

Dart Goblin (3 elixir) at bridge: 3 shots before reaction (459 damage). Even if Logged, you got damage AND baited Log. Next card (Skeleton Barrel/Goblin Gang) connects because their Log is gone. Dart Goblin is not a support card — it is a secondary win condition.

### 6. Goblin Gang Surround on Defense

Hog Rider approaching. Goblin Gang placed surrounding Hog: 5 units, 326 DPS. Hog dies in 6 seconds. Placed in front: 2-3 units reach, 130-195 DPS. Hog lives 2 extra seconds (636 extra tower damage). Surround every time.

### 7. Fireball Prediction on Their Mortar Defense

When your Mortar is locking onto their tower and they have a pattern (always Valkyrie at bridge), Fireball the bridge area predictively. Mortar shell + Fireball = 525 damage to Valkyrie (1,654 HP). She reaches Mortar at 1,129 HP. Mortar gets 3 more shots (660 damage). Valkyrie at 469 HP. Dies to Knight. Your Mortar survives full deploy.

### 8. Knight Placement: Between Mortar and Threat

Offensive Mortar at bridge. Enemy drops Mini P.E.K.K.A on Mortar. Knight between Mini P.E.K.K.A and Mortar. Knight tanks Mini P.E.K.K.A swings. Mortar continues shooting tower. Knight costs 3. Mini P.E.K.K.A costs 4. Knight survives with 30% HP after Mini P.E.K.K.A dies. Net: +1 trade, Mortar lives.

### 9. Bait Tracking: Know When Their Log Is Available

Their Log cycle: 4 cards in a 3.5 avg deck = 14 elixir = 39 seconds. After they Log: you have 39 seconds of Log-free window. Play Skeleton Barrel, Goblin Gang, Dart Goblin, Princess — all in this window. When 39 seconds pass, have bait cards ready but do not commit until they Log again.

### 10. Mortar Lock = Commit to Protecting It

Once Mortar locks onto tower, your entire gameplan shifts to PROTECTING the Mortar. Knight in front. Goblin Gang on defense. Log their swarm. Fireball their support. Let your tower take some damage — Mortar is dealing 220 damage every 5 seconds to their tower. Over 30 seconds: 6 shots, 1,320 tower damage. Protecting Mortar > protecting tower.
