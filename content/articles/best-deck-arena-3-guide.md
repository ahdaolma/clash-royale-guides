## Arena 3 Unlocks: Barbarians, Cannon, and Battle Ram

![Arena 3 deck Clash Royale](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Arena 3 (Barbarian Bowl, 800-1200 trophies) gives you four new cards that fundamentally change deck building: **Barbarians (5 elixir), Cannon (3), Battle Ram (4), and Barbarian Hut (7)**. Barbarians are the first true tank-killer swarm — five units dealing 138 DPS each for 690 total DPS at tournament standard. That shreds a Giant in under 5 seconds.

Battle Ram is your first "bridge spam" win condition. It charges after 4 tiles, dealing 341 charge damage and spawning two Barbarians on impact. At Arena 3, most opponents do not know how to counter it efficiently — they waste 5+ elixir on a 4-elixir card.

## Deck #1: Battle Ram Bridge Spam (61% Win Rate)

This is the highest win rate deck in Arena 3 according to RoyaleAPI data (15,000+ tracked matches, June 2026). The gameplan is simple: force your opponent to spend elixir on one lane, then Battle Ram the other.

### Core Strategy: The Dual Lane Split

The most powerful Arena 3 technique: when your opponent overcommits on one lane (e.g., Giant + Witch, 10 elixir), defend with Barbarians (5 elixir) on that side, then immediately Battle Ram (4 elixir) the opposite lane. They have zero elixir to defend. The Battle Ram connects for 341 charge damage + Barbarian hits — often 800+ tower damage from a 4-elixir card.

### Battle Ram Timing Windows

**Window 1 (0:30-0:45):** After their first push fails. Most Arena 3 players open with Giant + support. Defend, then counter-push opposite lane.

**Window 2 (1:30-1:45):** During their second push. They have less elixir this time. Split push.

**Window 3 (Double Elixir):** You can now chain Battle Rams. Play one, cycle Skeletons + Arrows, play another. Two Battle Rams in 10 seconds is nearly impossible to defend at this trophy range.

## Deck #2: Barbarian Hut Control (58% Win Rate)

### Barbarian Hut Placement

Always place Barbarian Hut **4 tiles from the river, centered**. This ensures Barbarians from both spawns walk toward the same lane. If placed off-center, Barbarians split lanes and are easily countered individually.

### Defending After Hut Placement

You spend 7 elixir on Hut. Your opponent will immediately push. Your defense sequence:
1. Cannon (3 elixir) placed 4-3 from the river, center — pulls Giant/Hog/Battle Ram
2. Skeletons (1 elixir) for distraction
3. By now your first Barbarians spawn and help defend

This sequence costs 4 elixir (Cannon + Skeletons) and stops most Arena 3 pushes cold.

### The Barbarian Surround

When a Giant or Giant Skeleton crosses your bridge, do not place Barbarians in front of it. Place them **surrounding the tank** — one tile behind, left, right, and slightly forward. All five Barbarians connect simultaneously, dealing 690 DPS. A level 6 Giant (2,600 HP) dies in 3.8 seconds.

## Matchup Guide

**Vs. Balloon:** Cannon placed 4-3 from the river, center. It pulls Balloon. Musketeer finishes it. Total defense cost: 7 elixir vs their 5 elixir Balloon... but you have a full-health Cannon for their next push. Net: even trade with building advantage.

**Vs. Witch Giant:** Fireball the Witch. Barbarians on the Giant. Valkyrie cleans surviving skeletons. This is a +2 elixir trade (your 13 vs their 15, plus surviving troops).

**Vs. Goblin Barrel:** Arrows instantly. Do not hesitate. Each Goblin deals 52 DPS — three Goblins on your tower for 3 seconds = 468 damage. Arrows cost 3 and deny all of it.
