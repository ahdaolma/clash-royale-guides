## Clash Royale Evolution Tier List — June 2026

![Evolution cards tier list](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Evolution cards (introduced June 2023) are enhanced versions of existing cards that cycle every 2-3 plays. Each evolution has a cycle count — you must play the base card N times before the evolved version appears. Evolutions add game-changing abilities: healing, multi-troop spawns, knockback, area damage, or stat boosts.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026) shows evolutions have a 56% average win rate when used — 4% above non-evolution decks. Running at least one evolution in your deck is now mandatory above 5,000 trophies. Running two is optimal at 7,000+.

## S Tier: Meta-Defining (Must-Use If Available)

### Knight Evolution (59% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)
![image](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Knight Evo gains a shield equal to 60% of his max HP AND heals 30% of damage dealt. At level 11: 1,988 HP + 1,193 shield = 3,181 effective HP for 3 elixir. That is 1,060 HP per elixir — higher than Giant (669 HP/elixir). He heals ~60 HP per swing, out-sustaining most 4-elixir troops.

**Why S tier:** 3 elixir, 2-cycle (appears every 3rd Knight), fits in any deck. Best value-per-elixir evolution in the game. Used in 34% of top-10,000 decks.

### Firecracker Evolution (58% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Firecracker Evo's rockets deal 40% more damage AND leave fire pools that deal 80 DPS for 3 seconds. At the bridge, she deals 224 damage per shot + fire pools covering a 3-tile area. Against swarm pushes: rockets kill everything + fire pools kill whatever survives. Against towers: fire pools add ~240 tower damage per shot.

**Why S tier:** 3 elixir, 2-cycle, splash + area denial. Single-handedly wins against swarm decks. Banned in 22% of Grand Challenge matches (highest ban rate).

## A Tier: Strong, Consistent, Versatile

### Skeletons Evolution (57% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)
![image](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Skeletons Evo: 4 skeletons instead of 3, AND every skeleton that hits a troop spawns another skeleton (up to 8 total). Against a Giant: 4 skeletons hit → 4 more spawn → 8 skeletons. Those 8 hit → 8 more spawn IF there is space. Potential: 3 → 12-20 skeletons from a 1-elixir card. Shreds tanks for +6 elixir trades.

**Why A tier:** 1 elixir, 2-cycle, potentially infinite value against tanks. Downside: dies to Zap/Log like regular Skeletons. If they have small spell, value drops.

### Bomber Evolution (55% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Bomber Evo: bounces twice (hits 3 times total), dealing 298 × 3 = 894 total area damage. Bounces cover a 6-tile line. Against Beatdown pushes: one Bomber Evo bounce clears all support troops behind the tank. Against Graveyard: bounce clears half the skeletons.

**Why A tier:** 2 elixir, 2-cycle, 894 area damage for 2 elixir. Destroys ground-based pushes. Downside: dies to any spell, can't target air.

### Royal Recruits Evolution (54% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Royal Recruits Evo: 6 Recruits with shields (244 HP each) that charge after 2 tiles, dealing double damage on charge hit. 6 × 138 × 2 (charge) = 1,656 burst damage if all charge. Shields absorb 6 hits. Total effective HP: 6 × (532+244) = 4,656 for 7 elixir.

**Why A tier:** 7 elixir, massive HP wall, split-lane pressure built in. Hard to counter efficiently. Downside: 7 elixir commitment.

## B Tier: Good in Specific Decks

### Tesla Evolution (53% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Tesla Evo: every 3rd attack releases a shockwave (192 area damage) that stuns for 0.5s. 192 DPS base + 64 DPS from shockwaves (every 4.8 seconds) = 256 effective DPS. Shockwaves clear swarms around Tesla, preventing surround kills.

**Why B tier:** 4 elixir, 2-cycle, strong against Hog and Balloon. Shockwave clears Bats/Skeletons that would normally swarm Tesla. Downside: Earthquake still counters, shockwave doesn't hit air.

### Battle Ram Evolution (52% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Battle Ram Evo: every 3rd hit, the Ram stuns nearby enemies for 0.5s. Barbarians spawned also have the stun ability. Against defensive buildings: stun interrupts building attacks. Against Inferno Tower: stun resets beam continuously. Ram becomes unstoppable.

**Why B tier:** counters Inferno Tower (Ram's traditional hard counter). Downside: 4 elixir, 2-cycle, still dies to P.E.K.K.A/Minion Horde.

## C Tier: Niche, Meta-Dependent

### Wall Breakers Evolution (50% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Wall Breakers Evo: 3 Wall Breakers instead of 2 (50% more damage). Third Breaker adds 220 damage. Total: 660 tower damage for 2 elixir. That is 330 damage per elixir — highest in the game.

**Why C tier:** damage ceiling is high, but dies to any spell. Log kills all 3. Predictable.

### Goblin Barrel Evolution (49% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Goblin Barrel Evo: spawns 4 Goblins instead of 3, and they have 30% more HP (220 HP vs 169). Survive Zap (192 damage). Must be Logged or Arrowed.

**Why C tier:** survives Zap, more damage. Downside: still dies to Log, and most decks at 6,000+ run Log specifically for Barrel.

---

## D Tier: Outclassed, Avoid

### Wizard Evolution (44% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Wizard Evo: gains a shield equal to 20% of max HP (144 HP at level 11). One extra hit of survival. Not enough to justify 5 elixir and 2-cycle cost. Regular Wizard is already overpriced; Evo Wizard fixes none of his problems.

### Mortar Evolution (45% Win Rate, 2-Cycle)

Mortar Evo: goblins spawn from Mortar shots at the target location. Each shot spawns 2 Spear Goblins. Over 30 seconds: 6 shots × 2 = 12 Spear Goblins. Interesting but too slow. Opponent clears Goblins before they accumulate.

## How to Choose Your Evolutions

**For Cycle decks (2.6-3.1 avg):** Knight + Skeletons. Both 2-cycle, cheap, defensive powerhouses.

**For Beatdown decks (3.8-4.5 avg):** Bomber + Royal Recruits. Bomber clears support, Recruits apply pressure.

**For Siege decks (3.0-3.5 avg):** Tesla + Knight. Tesla protects X-Bow/Mortar, Knight tanks.

**For Bridge Spam (3.5-3.9 avg):** Battle Ram + Firecracker. Ram stuns defenses, Firecracker clears swarms.
