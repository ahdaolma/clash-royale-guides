## 10 Hog Cycle Tips for Fast Cycle Domination

![Hog Rider cycle deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.MBQiMthUgwJiWx7WqqEA1AHaD7&f=1)

Hog Cycle (especially the classic Hog 2.6) is one of the highest-skill-ceiling decks in Clash Royale. The average elixir is 2.6 — meaning you cycle through your entire deck in 10.4 elixir. In double elixir, that is approximately every 7 seconds. Speed is your weapon. But speed without precision is just fast losing.

---

### 1. The Ice Golem + Hog Combo: Kite First, Then Push

Standard: Ice Golem (2) at the bridge, Hog Rider (4) behind. But the real tip: use Ice Golem on DEFENSE first. Kite their Mega Knight/Minion Horde to the opposite lane with Ice Golem. Once Ice Golem dies (or is about to), immediately Hog Rider same lane. The opponent just spent 4-7 elixir dealing with your 2-elixir Ice Golem. They have nothing for Hog. This sequence — defend with Ice Golem → Hog punish — is the most common winning pattern in Hog 2.6 above 6,000 trophies.

### 2. Cannon Placement: The 4-2 Plant

Cannon (3 elixir) at the 4-2 tile position (4 tiles from river, 2 from center) is the optimal defensive building placement in Hog Cycle. From this position, Cannon: pulls both-lane tanks, stays out of spell range from the bridge, and has line-of-sight to support troops. Against Hog Rider mirror: Cannon at 4-2 pulls their Hog into BOTH Princess Towers + Cannon. Their Hog dies before landing a single hit. +1 elixir trade (3 for 4) AND zero tower damage.

### 3. Pre-Plant Musketeer for Instant Value

Musketeer (4 elixir) has 6-tile range. Place her behind your King Tower 2 seconds BEFORE you expect their push. When their tank crosses the bridge, Musketeer is already positioned and firing. The pre-plant saves you 1.5 seconds of walk time = 2-3 extra shots. Against a 6-elixir Giant, those 3 extra shots (654 damage) can be the difference between the Giant reaching your tower (with 200 HP) and dying at the bridge. Pre-plant is risk-free when you have Cannon in cycle as backup.

### 4. The 1-Elixir Skeletons: Highest Skill Card in the Deck

Skeletons (1 elixir) are not cycle filler — they are a top-3 defensive card in the game when used correctly. They: fully counter Bandit (she dashes through one, the other two kill her), fully counter Mega Minion (surround placement), fully counter Musketeer at the bridge (surround, she shoots one per 1.1 seconds, dies before killing all three), and deal 324 DPS to distracted tanks. The surround placement — tapping 2 tiles BEHIND the target so Skeletons form a triangle — triples their survival time.

### 5. Fireball + Log: The 6-Elixir Tower Lock

In double elixir, Fireball (4) + Log (2) is 6 elixir that deals 308 tower damage and kills any ground swarm. When their tower is below 500 HP, cycle Fireball + Log exclusively. Do not push with Hog — you risk feeding them elixir from a positive defense. Spell cycle is guaranteed damage. In Hog 2.6, you can cycle back to Fireball every ~10 seconds in double elixir. Two Fireball cycles = ~600 tower damage. Unavoidable. Unstoppable. The most hated way to lose in Clash Royale — and the most reliable way to win.

### 6. Opposite-Lane Pressure Wins Mirror Matches

Hog Cycle mirror (Hog 2.6 vs Hog 2.6): the player who pressures BOTH lanes wins. When they Hog left, you defend with Cannon (3) + Skeletons (1) = 4 elixir for 4. But then immediately Hog RIGHT. Their Cannon is out of cycle (just used it). Your Hog connects. The key: track their Cannon cycle. When Cannon is the 3rd or 4th card in their hand, Hog the SAME lane — they cannot cycle to Cannon fast enough.

### 7. Ice Spirit + Hog: The 5-Elixir Bridge Rush

Ice Spirit (1) at the bridge + Hog (4) immediately behind. Ice Spirit reaches the tower first. If they drop Skeleton Army, Ice Spirit freezes ALL Skeletons, Hog walks past. If they drop Mini P.E.K.K.A, Ice Spirit freezes it for 1 second — Hog gets 1-2 extra swings. Total cost: 5 elixir. Average damage when countered: ~300. Average damage when unanswered: ~1,200. The 5-elixir Hog + Ice Spirit bridge rush is the most efficient offensive play in Clash Royale.

### 8. Defend Golem Without Overcommitting

Golem (8 elixir) vs Hog 2.6: the classic "bad matchup." The secret: Cannon + Musketeer + Ice Spirit kills Golem + support for 8 elixir. Cannon (3) pulls Golem. Musketeer (4) snipes support from 6 tiles away. Ice Spirit (1) freezes the push for 1 second, buying an extra Musketeer shot. Meanwhile, Hog Rider opposite lane forces them to split elixir between offense and defense. They spent 8 on Golem + 4-6 on support. You spent 8 on defense + 4 on Hog. You win because their tower takes damage and yours does not.

### 9. The 2.6 Cycle Magic Number

10.4 elixir = one full cycle. In single elixir: ~29 seconds per cycle. In double elixir: ~14.5 seconds. Your Hog appears every cycle. Their best Hog counter (building, Tornado, Mini P.E.K.K.A) appears every 4 cards = every cycle. This means: if you Hog and they counter, CYCLE AGGRESSIVELY. Drop Skeletons (1), Ice Spirit (1), Log (2), Skeletons (1) = 5 elixir, 4 cards cycled, Hog is back in hand. They have not cycled back to their counter yet. Second Hog connects. This is the "out-cycle" — the reason Hog 2.6 wins against heavier decks.

### 10. Know When to Stop Hogging

In the final 30 seconds, if you are up one tower: stop playing Hog Rider. Defend. Cycle spells. The Hog becomes a liability — it costs 4 elixir and gives them a counter-push opportunity. Up a tower with 30 seconds left, your win condition becomes Fireball. Every 10 seconds, Fireball their tower. Defend with Cannon + Musketeer + Skeletons + Ice Spirit. They cannot break through 2.6's defense with 30 seconds on the clock. The most common way to lose with Hog Cycle: overextending when ahead.

## Key Takeaway

Hog Cycle wins through speed, not power. Out-cycle their counters. Pressure both lanes. Spell cycle for the finish. Every 1-elixir card is a defensive tool, not just cycle filler. Play fast, but never out of control.
