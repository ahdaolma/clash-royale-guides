## Why Royal Giant Dominates Mid-Ladder

![Royal Giant deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Royal Giant costs 6 elixir. At tournament standard (level 11): 2,825 HP, 184 DPS, 6.5 range, targets buildings only. His 6.5 range is the key — it outranges Cannon (5.5), Bomb Tower (6.0), and most defensive buildings. Royal Giant starts shooting a tower the instant he crosses the bridge. Most opponents react by placing a building — but RG is already locked on and has fired 1-2 shots (184-368 damage) before their building even deploys.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026): Royal Giant has a 56% win rate at 4,000-6,000 trophies — the highest of any 6-elixir win condition. Above 7,000, his win rate drops to 51% as players learn to counter him. At mid-ladder, he is the most straightforward path to consistent wins.

### Royal Giant Threat Profile

| Stat | Value | Advantage |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Elixir | 6 | Cheaper than Golem (8), E-Giant (8) |
| HP | 2,825 | Survives Rocket (1,232) with 56% HP |
| DPS | 184 | 2 shots before building deploys = 368 damage |
| Range | 6.5 | Outranges Cannon, Bomb Tower, Tesla (hides but same range) |
| Targets | Buildings | Ignores troops — goes straight for tower |

## Deck #1: Royal Giant Furnace (The Classic)

Furnace (4 elixir) is RG's best friend. Placed in the same lane, Furnace chips the tower (178 per Fire Spirit wave × 7 waves = 1,246 damage over 50 seconds) while Royal Giant forces an answer. The opponent must choose: spend elixir countering Fire Spirits (leaving RG unopposed) or spend elixir countering RG (taking 1,246 chip damage). Most cannot afford both.

### The Deck

| Card | Elixir | Role |
|------|--------|------|
| Royal Giant | 6 | Win condition |
| Furnace | 4 | Lane pressure / chip damage |
| Mega Minion | 3 | Air DPS behind RG |
| Electro Spirit | 1 | Cycle / reset / chain stun |
| Barbarians | 5 | Tank defense |
| Zap | 2 | Reset Inferno / swarm clear |
| Fireball | 4 | Medium spell / Wizard killer |
| Archers | 3 | Air defense / cycle |

**Average elixir: 3.5**

### RG + Furnace Pressure Cycle

1. Furnace at 4-2 (4 tiles from river, 2 from tower — 1 tile closer than standard to ensure Fire Spirits reach).
2. RG at the bridge, same lane.
3. Fire Spirits chip tower while RG tanks.
4. Opponent responds to RG (Inferno Tower, P.E.K.K.A, Barbarians).
5. You Zap/Zap Spirit their defense.
6. RG gets 3-4 shots. Furnace has sent 2 waves of Fire Spirits (356 tower damage).
7. RG dies. Furnace still active — 5 more waves (890 more damage).
8. Cycle back to RG. Repeat.

Each full cycle: ~1,200 tower damage. Two cycles = tower dead.

## Deck #2: Royal Giant Lightning (Spell Cycle)

This variant runs Lightning (6 elixir) instead of Fireball. Lightning hits the 3 highest-HP targets for 996 damage each. Against the typical RG counter — Musketeer (656 HP) + Cannon (844 HP) + tower — Lightning kills Musketeer, leaves Cannon at 0 HP (844 < 996), and chips the tower.

### The Deck

| Card | Elixir | Role |
|------|--------|------|
| Royal Giant | 6 | Win condition |
| Lightning | 6 | Support eraser |
| Mega Minion | 3 | Air DPS |
| Electro Wizard | 4 | Stun / reset |
| Barbarians | 5 | Defense |
| Zap | 2 | Small spell |
| Ice Spirit | 1 | Cycle |
| Archers | 3 | Air defense |

**Average elixir: 3.8**

### Lightning Timing

RG crosses bridge. They place Musketeer + Cannon (8 elixir total). Lightning (6) hits Musketeer (dies), Cannon (dies), Tower (996 chip). RG now faces zero defense. Gets 5-6 shots (920-1,104 damage). Total push damage: 996 (Lightning tower) + 920-1,104 (RG) = 1,916-2,100. Nearly a full tower from one push. Cost: 12 elixir (RG 6 + Lightning 6). Opponent spent 8 elixir on defense that died. You spent 4 more to kill their defense AND chip tower. Massive value.

## Anti-Building Tech

### Against Inferno Tower: Zap at 2 Seconds

Inferno Tower ramps to 1,760 DPS. Zap (2 elixir) after 2 seconds of beam resets to 70 DPS. RG takes ~400 damage instead of ~2,000. RG survives with 85% HP and continues shooting tower/Inferno.

### Against Cannon: RG Outranges It

Cannon has 5.5 range. RG has 6.5. Place RG at the bridge. Cannon placed reactively at 4-3 is 5 tiles from RG — within RG's range. RG shoots Cannon from outside Cannon's range. Cannon dies in 5 shots (5 × 184 = 920 vs 844 HP). RG takes zero damage from Cannon.

### Against Tesla: Fireball the Tesla

Tesla hides underground — immune to spells when hidden. But Tesla MUST surface to attack RG. Fireball the Tesla as it surfaces. Tesla has 1,012 HP at level 11. Fireball (305) + RG 1 shot (184) = 489. 3 RG shots while Tesla is up: 552. Total: 489 + 552 = 1,041 > 1,012. Tesla dies. RG continues to tower.

## The Fisherman Variant (Higher Skill)

Fisherman (3 elixir) pulls enemy troops toward him. Against defensive P.E.K.K.A placed on RG: Fisherman pulls P.E.K.K.A AWAY from RG. RG continues shooting tower. Fisherman + RG = 9 elixir. P.E.K.K.A = 7. They spent 7, you spent 3 to neutralize it (RG cost separate — he was played anyway). Net: +4 trade.

Fisherman also pulls defending troops (Musketeer, Wizard, Hunter) into melee range where his 198 DPS kills them. The Fisherman variant has a higher skill ceiling but a 58% win rate above 6,000 trophies.

## Upgrade Priority

1. **Royal Giant** — damage per shot: 184 → 202 per level. 5 shots at level 9 = 920. 5 shots at level 11 = 1,010. Difference: one tower in 12 vs 13 shots.
2. **Furnace** — Fire Spirits MUST reach tower. At -1 level, tower one-shots them. Zero chip damage.
3. **Lightning** — one-shot Musketeer/Wizard breakpoint at equal level.
4. **Zap** — Goblin kill breakpoint at equal level.
