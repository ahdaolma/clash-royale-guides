## What Is PEKKA Bridge Spam?

PEKKA Bridge Spam is a hybrid archetype that combines heavy defense with relentless bridge pressure. You defend with PEKKA (7 elixir) and immediately convert the surviving PEKKA into a counter-push with cheap bridge-spam cards.

The standard 2026 PEKKA Bridge Spam:

- **PEKKA** (7 elixir) ? Tank killer, counter-push threat
- **Battle Ram** (4 elixir) ? Win condition, pressure
- **Bandit** (3 elixir) ? Bridge pressure, dash mechanic
- **Royal Ghost** (3 elixir) ? Stealth chip, splash
- **Magic Archer** (4 elixir) ? Geometry shots, tower chip
- **Electro Wizard** (4 elixir) ? Stun, reset, air defense
- **Poison** (4 elixir) ? Area denial, Graveyard counter
- **Zap** (2 elixir) ? Reset, swarm clear

Average elixir: 3.9. This deck is a scalpel ? precise, punishing, and demanding of timing.

## The Bridge Spam Rhythm

Bridge Spam wins by never letting your opponent breathe. The rhythm:

1. Defend their push with minimal elixir (PEKKA if needed, E-Wiz + Ghost if possible)
2. The moment their push dies, play Battle Ram at the bridge in the OPPOSITE lane
3. Bandit follows 1 second later in the same lane
4. They now have 6 elixir to defend two threats in two lanes
5. Magic Archer at the bridge to snipe their defensive troops through their tower

This sequence ? defend, split pressure, snipe ? repeats every 20 seconds. Your opponent is on the back foot the entire match.

## PEKKA: When to Use and When to Save

PEKKA costs 7 elixir. Deploying her at the wrong time loses the game. Deploying her at the right time wins it.

**Use PEKKA when:**
- Opponent plays Golem, Giant, Royal Giant, or Mega Knight
- You have 8+ elixir and they just committed 7+ to a tank
- You need to kill their win condition AND start a counter-push

**Save PEKKA when:**
- They are playing Hog Cycle (PEKKA is too slow to catch Hog)
- They have swarm cards ready (PEKKA attacks one at a time and gets overwhelmed)
- You are at an elixir disadvantage (you need 3+ elixir to support a PEKKA push)

A PEKKA that kills a Golem and walks to their tower with half HP is the best 7 elixir you will ever spend.

## Magic Archer Geometry

Magic Archer's arrows pierce through the first target and travel 11 tiles. When placed at the bridge and aimed at a troop behind the tower, the arrows hit the tower AND the troop. This is called "geometry."

**The Standard Geometry Shot:**
- Place Magic Archer at the bridge, aligned with their tower
- Their defensive troop (Musketeer, Wizard) is placed behind the tower
- Magic Archer shoots the troop. The arrow pierces and hits the tower.
- Damage: 157 to troop + 157 to tower per shot. At 1.1 second fire rate, 5 shots = 785 tower damage from a 4-elixir card they cannot reach.

**The Double Geometry (Pro):**
- Opponent places two troops behind two towers (split lane defense)
- Place Magic Archer slightly off-center at the bridge
- His arrows travel diagonally through both troops into both towers
- This is rare but game-winning

## Bandit Dashing

Bandit's dash makes her invulnerable for the dash duration. Time her deploy so she dashes into their defensive troop the moment they place it. She dashes, takes zero damage during the dash, and immediately starts attacking. Against a Wizard or Musketeer, Bandit wins 1v1 if she dashes first.

**The Bridge Dash:**
Deploy Bandit at the bridge. She dashes immediately into the tower (4 tiles range). She gets 1-2 hits before they react. 256 damage per hit. Free chip damage whenever you have 3 elixir and they are distracted.

## Matchup Guide

### Vs. Golem Beatdown (Favorable)
PEKKA eats Golem. Bandit + Battle Ram punish opposite lane. They cannot Golem when PEKKA is in your hand. This is a hard counter matchup.

### Vs. Log Bait (Even)
Poison kills Princess and Goblin Barrel. Zap for Skeleton Army. PEKKA for Knight. But their cycle is faster ? stay disciplined.

### Vs. Hog 2.6 (Unfavorable)
PEKKA is too slow. Hog gets 1-2 swings before you can respond. Battle Ram is your best defense ? it blocks Hog pathing. Bandit + Ghost pressure keeps them from cycling freely.

### Vs. LavaLoon (Unfavorable)
Only Electro Wizard hits air consistently. Poison for Lava Pups. Magic Archer snipes Balloon. But against a determined LavaLoon push, you lack air DPS. Defend perfectly or lose.

### Vs. X-Bow (Favorable)
PEKKA in front of X-Bow blocks all shots for 20+ seconds. Battle Ram smashes X-Bow. Bandit dashes through their defense. Poison the X-Bow placement zone. This is another hard counter.

## Pro Tips

1. **Never lead with PEKKA.** PEKKA behind King Tower is a signal to your opponent: "Rush the other lane for free tower damage." Only PEKKA reactively.

2. **Bandit-Zap synchronization.** Time Zap to reset Inferno Tower/Inferno Dragon the moment Bandit dashes. The reset gives Bandit 2 extra hits.

3. **Poison + Magic Archer.** Poison denies their defensive building while Magic Archer shoots through their troops into the tower. This combo is devastating against Cannon/Tesla users.

4. **Battle Ram placement.** Place Ram 1 tile from the edge of the arena. It charges at a slight angle, making it harder to pull with buildings.

5. **The Ghost Pump Fake.** Deploy Royal Ghost at the bridge. Your opponent expects a push and commits elixir to defend. Ghost goes invisible, wastes their elixir, and you have a 3-elixir advantage.
