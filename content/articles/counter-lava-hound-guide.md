## Why Lava Hound Is the Silent Tower-Killer

![Lava Hound counter guide](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Lava Hound costs 7 elixir. At tournament standard: 3,280 HP, 53 DPS (negligible), flying, and on death spawns 6 Lava Pups (each 216 HP, 67 DPS) — 1,296 total Pup HP and 402 combined Pup DPS. The Hound itself deals almost no damage. The real threat is: (a) Lava Pups after death, and (b) the support behind it (Balloon, Skeleton Dragons, Mega Minion).

LavaLoon (Lava Hound + Balloon + support, ~15 elixir) has been a top-3 tournament archetype since 2017. The win condition is NOT the Lava Hound — it is the Balloon behind the Hound. The Hound tanks for Balloon. Your air defense targets the Hound (closest). Balloon flies past and reaches your tower.

RoyaleAPI data: Lava Hound has a 55% win rate at 4,000-6,000 trophies, rising to 58% when paired with Balloon. The counter is two-part: kill the Balloon AND clear the Pups. Fail either and you lose a tower.

### Lava Hound Threat Profile

| Phase | HP | DPS | Threat |
|-------|-----|-----|--------|
| Lava Hound (body) | 3,280 | 53 | Minimal — it is a flying tank, not a damage dealer |
| Lava Pups × 6 | 216 each (1,296 total) | 67 each (402 total) | The real threat — 402 DPS on tower if uncleared |
| Support (typical: Balloon) | 1,640 | 1,248/bomb | The tower-killer hiding behind Hound |

## Tier S Counters: Handling LavaLoon (The Full Push)

### The LavaLoon Defense Sequence (12-15 Elixir Push)

1. Lava Hound at the back (7 elixir). You push opposite lane immediately — Hog Rider, Battle Ram, Wall Breakers. Forces elixir spending.
2. Lava Hound crosses bridge. Balloon (5 elixir) placed behind it.
3. Your defense: Inferno Tower at 4-3 center locks onto Lava Hound. Mega Minion targets Balloon (NOT the Hound — the Balloon is the real threat).
4. Tornado pulls Balloon AWAY from your tower, toward the center. Mega Minion + Inferno Tower + 2 towers = Balloon dead in 3 seconds.
5. Lava Hound dies. 6 Pups spawn. Arrows (3 elixir) kills all 6 Pups instantly (216 HP each < 305 Arrows damage).
6. Push denied. Total defense: 5 (Inferno) + 3 (Mega Minion) + 3 (Tornado) + 3 (Arrows) = 14 elixir vs their 12+ elixir push. Slightly negative on elixir, but tower is safe and you have Inferno Tower + Mega Minion alive for counter-push.

### Executioner + Tornado: The 8-Elixir LavaLoon Wipe

Executioner (5 elixir) + Tornado (3 elixir): the strongest anti-air combo in the game. Executioner's axe deals 187×2 (forward + return) area damage to BOTH air and ground. Tornado pulls Lava Hound, Balloon, and any support into one tile. Axe hits everything twice.

Against Lava Hound (3,280 HP) + Balloon (1,640 HP): 374 damage per axe pass. 5 axe passes = 1,870 damage to both. Neither dies from Executioner alone. BUT: + two towers (224 DPS). Over 10 seconds: 1,870 + 2,240 = 4,110 total damage to the push. Lava Hound dies. Balloon dies. Pups spawn directly into axe's return hit — all 6 die. Total defense: 8 elixir vs 12+. +4 trade.

## Tier A: Individual Counters

### Inferno Tower (5 elixir): Melts the Hound

Lava Hound has 3,280 HP. Inferno Tower ramps to 1,760 DPS. Hound dies in 2.5 seconds (beam averages ~1,300 DPS over that time). Pups spawn at Inferno Tower's location — Arrows immediately. 5 + 3 = 8 elixir. +1 trade vs Hound alone (7). Even vs Hound + Balloon (12 — because you still need Balloon defense).

### Mega Minion (3 elixir): Focus Balloon, Not Hound

The critical mistake: targeting Lava Hound with Mega Minion. Lava Hound has 53 DPS — it is not a threat. Balloon has 1,248 bomb damage — it is THE threat. Always Mega Minion on Balloon, ignoring the Hound entirely. Let your towers + building handle Hound.

Mega Minion (219 DPS) + two towers (224 DPS) = 443 DPS on Balloon. Balloon dies in 3.7 seconds. 3 elixir counters 5 elixir. +2 trade.

### Arrows (3 elixir): Pup Clearance

6 Lava Pups × 216 HP each. Arrows: 305 damage in 3 waves over 1.1 seconds. All 6 Pups die. 3 elixir clears the entire death spawn of a 7-elixir card. If you miss Arrows, 6 Pups deal 402 DPS to your tower — roughly 800 damage before the tower kills them. Arrows are mandatory in any deck facing Lava Hound.

## Tier B: Budget / Alternative Counters

### Wizard (5 elixir): Splash Everything

Wizard: 281 splash DPS at 5.5 range. Against Lava Hound + Balloon: Wizard splash hits both simultaneously (they are close together). DPS is split — 281 total, not per target. Not enough to kill Balloon before it reaches tower. BUT: Wizard + Tesla (4) = Tesla on Hound, Wizard on Balloon. Combined with towers: enough to stop the push. Wizard costs 5, Tesla costs 4 = 9 vs 12. Works, but Executioner is better for the same elixir.

### Hunter (4 elixir): Shotgun Air Defense

Hunter deals 69 × 10 pellets per shot at close range = 690 damage per shot. Against Balloon directly on top of him: 2 shots (1,380) + tower (112) = 1,492 — almost dead. Third shot kills. Hunter costs 4 vs Balloon's 5. +1 trade. BUT: Hunter is short-range (4 tiles) and squishy (756 HP). Dies to Fireball. Use only in Hunter-specific decks.

## The Opposite-Lane Punish Against Lava Hound

When opponent plays Lava Hound at the back (7 elixir, 3 remaining), immediately push opposite lane:
- Hog Rider + Ice Spirit (5 elixir): 318-636 tower damage
- E-Barbs + Rage (8 elixir): potential tower kill
- Wall Breakers + Miner (5 elixir): 440 damage

The Lava Hound player MUST respond with their 3 elixir. If they do not, they lose a tower. If they do, their Lava Hound reaches your side with zero support. A lone Lava Hound (53 DPS) is not a threat — any building + Arrows for Pups handles it.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1: Targeting Lava Hound instead of Balloon.** Lava Hound deals 53 DPS. Balloon deals 1,248. Always kill Balloon first. The Hound can wait.

**Mistake 2: No Arrows for Pups.** If your deck has no small spell (Arrows, Zap, Snowball), Lava Pups deal 800+ damage every push. Your tower dies by the third push. Arrows are MANDATORY against Lava Hound.

**Mistake 3: Placing air defense at the bridge.** Balloon is placed BEHIND Lava Hound. Air defense at the bridge targets the Hound (closer). Place air defense 4-5 tiles back so it targets Balloon as it passes the Hound.

**Mistake 4: Not punishing opposite lane.** Every second you do not attack opposite lane, the Lava Hound player accumulates elixir for support. Punish within 3 seconds of Hound placement.
