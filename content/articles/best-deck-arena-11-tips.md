## 10 Arena 11 Tips to Master Electro Valley

### 1. E-Giant Tornado: Pull Everything Into the Zap

E-Giant at the tower. They drop Skeleton Army (15 skeletons). Tornado pulls all 15 into E-Giant. Each skeleton attacks once → 15 × 192 zap = 2,880 damage. All skeletons die in 2 seconds. Tower also takes 4-6 zaps (768-1,152 damage). Total: their defense dead + tower chipped for 1,000+. Cost: 8 (E-Giant) + 3 (Tornado) = 11 elixir. Their defense cost: 3 (Skeleton Army). But: tower takes 1,000 damage AND they have no elixir left. This is a winning trade.

### 2. E-Giant Hard Counters Inferno Tower

Inferno Tower ramps to 1,760 DPS. Each tick triggers E-Giant's zap — 192 damage + 0.5s stun. The stun RESETS Inferno Tower's ramp. It starts over at 70 DPS. Zap again. Reset again. Inferno Tower never ramps past 300 DPS against E-Giant. The hard counter to every tank becomes useless. Do not fear Inferno Tower when you run E-Giant.

### 3. Sparky Is a Defense Card, Not an Attack Card

Sparky costs 6 elixir. Place her behind your King Tower and let her walk to the bridge. By the time she arrives, you have regenerated to 10 elixir and can support her. If you play Sparky at the bridge, you have 4 elixir — they Zap, you have nothing. Always play Sparky from the back.

### 4. Sparky + Goblin Giant: The 12-Elixir Push

Goblin Giant (6) at the back, Sparky (6) behind him. As they approach the bridge, you are at 7-8 elixir. Support: Dark Prince (4) or Electro Wizard (4) behind Sparky. Opponent must answer: a tank (3,200 HP), a 1,100-damage cannon, and splash support. Total defense needed: 12+ elixir. Most Arena 11 players have 8-10 elixir and lose a tower.

### 5. Electro Wizard on Goblin Barrel: Instant +1

Goblin Barrel lands. Drop Electro Wizard directly on top. Spawn damage: 192. Goblins have 169 HP. All three Goblins die instantly. You spent 4 elixir to counter 3, but you have a full-HP E-Wiz on the field. He walks to their tower, dealing 200+ damage before they answer. Net: even trade or better. Never Log a Barrel if you have E-Wiz in hand.

### 6. Zappies Stun-Lock: 4 Elixir Counters 6+ Elixir

Place Zappies (4 elixir) 5 tiles from the river, spread horizontally. Against a single tank (Giant, Golem, Royal Giant): each Zappie attacks every 1.1 seconds, staggering their shots. The tank is stunned 0.5s every 0.37 seconds — permanently stun-locked. It never reaches your tower. Against Sparky (6 elixir): Zappies reset her charge every 1.1 seconds. She never fires. 4 elixir counters 6 permanently. Against Balloon (5 elixir): Zappies stun prevents bomb drops. Tower kills Balloon before it drops a single bomb.

### 7. Electro Dragon Chain Breaks Pushes

Electro Dragon's chain hits 3 targets: 99 → 79 → 63 damage with 0.5s stun. Against Giant + Witch + Musketeer: chain hits all three. All three stunned. Giant stops walking. Witch stops spawning. Musketeer stops shooting. Every 1.7 seconds, all three are stunned again. Combined with tower damage, the push dies before reaching your tower. E-Dragon costs 5 elixir. The push costs 14. You win.

### 8. Lightning the Support, Not the Tank

When defending a beatdown push (Giant + Wizard + Musketeer), Light the SUPPORT troops, not the Giant. Lightning hits 3 highest-HP targets: Wizard (720), Musketeer (656), Princess Tower (2,534). Wizard and Musketeer die. Giant alone deals 211 DPS to your tower — easily tanked with any 3-elixir card. If you Lightning the Giant, the support troops survive and deal 218+281 = 499 combined DPS. Your tower melts. Always kill the DPS first.

### 9. E-Giant in Double Elixir: Cycle Him Twice

In double elixir (1 elixir per 1.4 seconds), you can cycle E-Giant every 17 seconds (8 elixir × 1.4 + 4 cycle cards ~5.6 = 17s). Two E-Giant pushes in 34 seconds. Most opponents can defend one E-Giant. Almost none can defend two back-to-back. The second E-Giant push connects while their defensive building/counters are still out of cycle.

### 10. P.E.K.K.A Is Your Hardest Counter — Know When to Switch

P.E.K.K.A (7 elixir) kills E-Giant in 6 swings (678 × 6 = 4,068 > 3,600 E-Giant HP). E-Giant zaps P.E.K.K.A for 192 per swing — negligible against P.E.K.K.A's 3,760 HP. If your opponent runs P.E.K.K.A, stop cycling E-Giant. Switch to Sparky + Goblin Giant — Sparky one-shots P.E.K.K.A from behind Goblin Giant. Or switch to spell cycle: Lightning their tower and support troops, defend with E-Wiz and Zappies, win in overtime.
