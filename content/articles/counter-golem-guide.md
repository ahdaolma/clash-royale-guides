## Why Golem Is a Win Condition You Must Respect

![Golem counter guide](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Golem costs 8 elixir — the most expensive win condition in Clash Royale. At tournament standard (level 11): 4,256 HP main body + 2 Golemites at 864 HP each + 346 death damage + 72 Golemite death damage = **5,984 total HP.** That is 748 HP per elixir — the highest in the game.

But the real threat is not Golem himself (211 DPS at tournament standard — less than a single Archer). It is the support behind him: Night Witch, Baby Dragon, Lumberjack, Mega Minion. A Golem push is a 16-20 elixir investment over 30 seconds. Your job: survive the push OR kill the support so Golem becomes a 211 DPS pillow.

RoyaleAPI data shows Golem has a 58% win rate in the 4,000-6,000 trophy range — but only 48% against players who punish the initial Golem placement. The difference: 10 percentage points, entirely based on the first 10 seconds after Golem is played.

### Golem Threat Profile

| Phase | HP | DPS | What You Face |
|-------|-----|-----|---------------|
| Golem (body) | 4,256 | 211 | Tank + death damage (346 area) |

## Tier S: Punish the Golem Placement (Win the Game in 10 Seconds)

The single most important Golem counter is the opposite-lane punish. When your opponent plays Golem (8 elixir) behind their King Tower, they have exactly 2 elixir remaining. You have 10 elixir. The question is not "how do I defend Golem?" — it is "can I take their tower before Golem reaches mine?"

### Elite Barbarians + Rage (8 elixir): The Classic Punish

E-Barbs (6) + Rage (2) at the opposite-lane bridge. They have 2 elixir. Best case: Skeleton Army (3), which dies to E-Barbs + tower zap in 1 second. Worst case: they have nothing. Tower dies in 4 seconds. You spent 8 elixir to take a tower while they spent 8 on a Golem that now has no tower to support.

**When this works:** Arena 6-12, where Golem players overcommit without elixir reserves. Above Legendary Arena, Golem players save Elixir Collector or cycle cards before playing Golem. Still worth the attempt — it forces them to always have a defensive reserve, limiting their Golem support.

### Hog Rider + Ice Spirit (5 elixir): The Safe Punish

Hog Rider (4) + Ice Spirit (1) opposite lane. Hog connects for 318-636 damage. Ice Spirit freezes their 2-elixir defense. Total cost: 5 elixir. You have 5 remaining for defense. Lower reward than E-Barbs, but much safer. This is the preferred punish above 4,000 trophies.

## Tier A: Building-Based Defense

### Inferno Tower (5 elixir): The Golem Melter

Inferno Tower is the single best building against Golem. Beam ramps to 1,760 DPS. Golem's 4,256 HP melts in 3.5 seconds. Golemites (864 each) die in 1.5 seconds each. Total time: 6.5 seconds. Inferno Tower costs 5 to counter 8. +3 trade.

**Placement:** 4-3 center. Pulls Golem to center where both towers shoot. Inferno beam locks onto Golem. Support troops (Night Witch, Baby Dragon) cannot reach Inferno Tower because it is pulled to center — they walk around the river into tower range first.

**Counter-play:** Zap/Electro Wizard resets Inferno beam. If they run Zap, place Ice Spirit or Skeletons in front of Inferno Tower to absorb the Zap. Or: play Tesla (4) behind Inferno — if Inferno gets Zapped, Tesla is still firing.

### Tesla (4 elixir): The Spell-Proof Alternative

Tesla hides underground — immune to spells. Golem takes Tesla (192 DPS) + 2 towers (224 DPS) = 416 DPS. Golem body dies in 10.2 seconds. Golemites die in 4.2 seconds. Total: 14.4 seconds. Slow, but Tesla costs 4 to counter 8. +4 trade. Tesla survives the entire push and is ready for the next one.

## Tier B: Tank Killer Troops

### P.E.K.K.A (7 elixir): The Golem Eater

P.E.K.K.A: 678 damage per swing, 1.8s attack speed. Golem body (4,256 HP): 7 swings × 678 = 4,746 damage. Golem dies in 7 swings (12.6 seconds). Golemites: 2 swings each. Total: 11 swings, 19.8 seconds. P.E.K.K.A costs 7 vs Golem's 8. +1 trade.

BUT: P.E.K.K.A is vulnerable to support troops. Night Witch Bats + Baby Dragon splash kill her before she kills Golem. You need your own support (Baby Dragon, Wizard, Executioner) to clear their support while P.E.K.K.A tanks Golem.

**P.E.K.K.A placement:** Behind your tower, so she walks toward Golem. She tanks support troops while walking. If placed at the bridge, she gets swarmed and dies.

### Inferno Dragon (4 elixir): Flying Inferno Tower

Inferno Dragon ramps to 1,400 DPS (slightly less than Inferno Tower's 1,760). Flying means ground troops (Night Witch, Dark Prince) cannot reach it. 4 elixir vs 8. +4 trade if unopposed. BUT: Zap/Electro Wizard reset the beam. Baby Dragon splashes it. Electro Dragon stuns it. Only use if you have Zap bait or they have no reset cards.

## Tier C: Spell Cycling (Win in Overtime)

### Rocket Cycle: Kill the Support, Chip the Tower

Against Golem decks that have overwhelming support (Night Witch + Baby Dragon + Lumberjack + Mega Minion), defending the Golem is impossible. Instead: Rocket their support AND their tower. 

Rocket (6 elixir, 1,232 damage) hits Golem + support + tower. Night Witch (1,012 HP) dies to Rocket. Baby Dragon (1,074 HP) dies. Lumberjack (1,100 HP) survives with 0 HP... actually 1,100 < 1,232, so Lumberjack dies too. Mega Minion (900 HP) dies. Rocket kills 3 support troops for 6 elixir. Their Golem continues to your tower alone (211 DPS). Drop any 3-elixir troop to tank. 

Then counter-push: you have surviving defense troops + they spent 8 on Golem + 8-12 on support. You win the elixir war.

## The Golem Defense Sequence

Here is the exact sequence to defend a full Golem push (Golem + Night Witch + Baby Dragon + Mega Minion, 20 elixir):

1. **0s: Golem placed.** Immediately Hog Rider opposite lane (4 elixir, 318-636 damage, forces response).
2. **15s: Golem crosses bridge.** Plant Inferno Tower at 4-3 center. Locks onto Golem (5 elixir → now at 3).
3. **18s: Support enters your side.** Rocket the Night Witch + Baby Dragon + Mega Minion (6 elixir → now at -3, but elixir regen keeps you at 2-3).
4. **22s: Golem dies.** Golemites spawn. Inferno Tower kills them. Support is dead from Rocket.
5. **30s: Push denied.** You spent 5+6=11 elixir + 4 for Hog = 15 total. They spent 20. +5 trade.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1: Defending the Golem instead of punishing.** If you see Golem at the back and do nothing for 15 seconds while it walks toward you, you lose. Always push opposite lane immediately.

**Mistake 2: Placing all defense at the bridge.** Golem support troops walk behind Golem. If you place Inferno Tower at the bridge, Night Witch walks past it and spawns Bats on your tower. Place defense at 4-3 or deeper.

**Mistake 3: Ignoring Golemite death damage.** Golemites each deal 72 death damage. Combined with Golem's 346, total death burst: 490 area damage. This kills half-health Musketeers, Wizards, and Archers. Space your defensive troops 3+ tiles from Golem when it is about to die.

**Mistake 4: Using small swarms against Golem.** Skeleton Army (3 elixir) deals 670 DPS to Golem but dies to Golem death damage (346). You spent 3 elixir, they lost nothing (Golem dies anyway). Use buildings or tank-killers, not swarms.
