## What Is Log Bait?

![Clash Royale card](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fthf.bing.com%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.0HvlX_Qs8Lyv8vj8v04heQHaGZ&f=1)


Log Bait is one of the oldest and most reliable archetypes in Clash Royale. The concept is simple: run multiple cards that demand a specific spell counter, forcing your opponent to choose which threat to answer. Their Log cannot stop both your Goblin Barrel AND your Princess AND your Dart Goblin.

The classic 2026 Log Bait deck:

- **Goblin Barrel** (3 elixir) ? Primary win condition
- **Princess** (3 elixir) ? Bridge pressure, bait, chip
- **Dart Goblin** (3 elixir) ? Secondary bait, high DPS
- **Goblin Gang** (3 elixir) ? Defense, split-lane pressure
- **Knight Evolution** (3 elixir) ? Mini tank, evolution cycle
- **Inferno Tower** (5 elixir) ? Tank killer
- **Rocket** (6 elixir) ? Spell cycle finisher, emergency defense
- **The Log** (2 elixir) ? Your small spell

Average elixir: 3.5. This is a cycle-heavy deck that wins through chip damage and spell cycling.

## The Bait Chain

The core mechanic: each bait card forces a response. If they Log your Princess at the bridge, you have a 3-second window where Goblin Barrel cannot be Logged. Understanding this chain is what separates good Log Bait players from great ones.

**The Sequence:**

1. **Princess at the bridge** (3 elixir) ? Forces Log or a troop response. If they Log, proceed to step 2.
2. **Goblin Barrel to the tower** (3 elixir) ? Their Log is out of cycle. Goblins get 2-3 stabs (200-300 damage).
3. **Dart Goblin** (3 elixir) ? Now they panic. Their Log just came back but they also need it for your next Barrel.
4. **Repeat from step 1** ? Their Log cycle cannot keep up with your three bait cards.

Your bait card cycle is 4 cards. Their Log cycle is 4 cards. If you sequence correctly, every other Barrel connects.

## Goblin Barrel Placements

### The Standard Barrel
Thrown directly at the tower. Easiest to Log. Use when you know their Log is out of cycle.

### The Tricky Barrel
Thrown 1 tile in front of the tower, slightly to the side. Harder for opponents to time their Log perfectly. Goblins spread out, so some survive even if the Log is slightly mistimed.

### The Deep Barrel
Thrown behind the tower. Goblins land 1 tile behind the tower. Hardest to react to because the shadow moves differently. If the opponent pre-Logs, they miss entirely.

### The Anti-Tornado Barrel
Thrown wide to the edge. Goblins land spread out, making Tornado pulls ineffective. Use against Tornado users.

## Matchup Guide

### Vs. Bridge Spam (Even)
Battle Ram and Bandit pressure is tough. Inferno Tower for tanks. Goblin Gang to surround Bandit. Do not Rocket cycle unless you have an elixir lead ? it is too expensive.

### Vs. Beatdown (Favorable)
Golem and Lava Hound are too slow. You cycle Barrels faster than they build pushes. Inferno Tower stops their tank. By double elixir, if they have not taken a tower, Rocket cycle finishes them.

### Vs. Siege (Favorable)
Knight tanks X-Bow shots. Rocket kills X-Bow in one hit. Princess pressures from the bridge. Mortar can be distracted with Goblin Gang.

### Vs. Miner Control (Unfavorable)
Miner chip is hard to stop. They have multiple small spells. Poison kills your bait cards. This is the hardest matchup.

### Vs. Mirror Match
Whoever has better spell tracking wins. Count their Log cycle. The player who connects more Barrels wins.

## Rocket Cycling: The Finisher

In double elixir, if your opponent tower is below 800 HP, switch to Rocket cycling. Rocket deals 591 Crown Tower damage at tournament standard. Two Rockets plus chip damage from Princess usually finishes a tower.

**When to Rocket cycle:**
- Tower is under 800 HP
- You have an elixir lead of 3+
- You are not under immediate tower threat
- Overtime has started (elixir flows faster)

## Defensive Combos

**Goblin Gang + Knight:** Surround a lone troop with Goblin Gang, Knight tanks for them. Kills most 5-elixir pushes for 6 elixir.

**Princess + Inferno Tower:** Princess at the bridge to pressure, Inferno Tower 4-3 placement for their tank. They cannot handle both lanes.

**Dart Goblin sniping:** Place Dart Goblin 6 tiles from a troop. His 6.5 range out-ranges everything except Princess and Magic Archer. He chips down tanks while your Inferno Tower ramps up.

## Pro Tips

![Clash Royale card](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fthf.bing.com%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.EjjgGxiyUa_78j2hUmPpmAHaDt&f=1)


1. **Count their spell cycle.** If you know their Log is 2 cards away, you can Barrel safely. After they use Log, count in your head: 1-2-3-4, Log is back.

2. **Princess in the other lane.** When they push one lane, drop Princess at the bridge in the opposite lane. She gets 3-4 shots uncontested. 420 damage free.

3. **Defensive Rocket.** Against a 10+ elixir push, Rocket can kill the support troops while your Inferno Tower handles the tank. Rocket deals 1512 damage ? it one-shots Wizard, Musketeer, Electro Wizard, Ice Wizard, Magic Archer, and Zappies.

4. **Evolution Knight placement.** Evo Knight walks while attacking. Place him 1 tile from the bridge and he tanks tower shots while walking forward, letting your Goblins deal damage safely.

5. **Never Barrel first.** Always bait a spell before committing Barrel. A naked Barrel against a Log user is a wasted 3 elixir.

## Key Takeaways

Log Bait rewards patience and pattern recognition. You win not by overpowering your opponent, but by forcing impossible choices. Master the bait chain, count their spell cycle, and your Goblin Barrels will connect. When they do not, Rocket finishes the job.

After 6 years in the meta, Log Bait remains viable because its core mechanic ? forcing hard choices ? never goes out of style.
