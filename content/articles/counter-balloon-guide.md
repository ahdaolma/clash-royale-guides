## Why Balloon Is So Deadly — And Why Most Players Counter It Wrong

![Balloon counter guide](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Balloon costs 5 elixir. At tournament standard (level 11), it has 1,640 HP, deals 1,248 damage per bomb, and drops a death bomb for 798 area damage. One connection = 1,248 tower damage. Two connections = 2,496 — nearly a full tower (2,534 HP). It is the single highest burst-damage win condition in Clash Royale.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026) shows Balloon appears in 22% of decks between 3,000-6,000 trophies, making it the second most common win condition after Hog Rider. In LavaLoon decks, it has a 55% win rate. Knowing how to counter it is not optional — it is mandatory.

### The Balloon Threat Profile

| Stat | Value | What It Means |
|------|-------|---------------|
| HP | 1,640 | Survives Fireball (305), Poison (810), Arrows (305), Zap (192) |
| Bomb damage | 1,248 | One-shots Musketeer, Wizard, Ice Wizard |
| Death damage | 798 | Kills all 4-elixir support troops at half HP |
| Speed | Medium | Reaches tower in 4.5 seconds from bridge |
| Targets | Buildings only | Ignores troops — you must kill it before it reaches tower |

## Tier S Counters: Positive Elixir Trades

### Rocket (6 elixir): One-Shot Kill

Rocket deals 1,232 damage at tournament standard. Balloon has 1,640 HP. Rocket does NOT one-shot Balloon. However: Rocket + 2 tower shots (112 each = 224) = 1,456. Still not enough. Rocket + 4 tower shots (448) = 1,680. Balloon dies. But that assumes Balloon takes 4 seconds to reach the tower.

**The real Rocket math:** Rocket + any troop hit kills Balloon. Rocket (1,232) + Mega Minion one swing (219) = 1,451. Still not. Rocket + Musketeer one shot (218) = 1,450. Two Musketeer shots = 1,668 > 1,640. Balloon dies before reaching tower.

**Rocket placement:** Aim Rocket so it hits Balloon and the approaching support (Lava Hound, Baby Dragon). 6 elixir to kill 5 elixir Balloon is a -1 trade, but if you also hit their 4-elixir Baby Dragon, it is a +3 trade. Rocket is a net positive when it hits multiple targets.

### Tornado (3 elixir): King Tower Activation

This is the single most important Balloon counter in the game. Tornado costs 3 elixir. If you pull Balloon into your King Tower, the King activates (112 DPS). From that point forward, every Balloon push takes damage from THREE towers instead of two. This reduces future Balloon damage by 35-50% for the rest of the match.

**Exact Tornado placement:** Place Tornado 2 tiles in front of your King Tower, centered. Balloon must be crossing the river on the outer lane. Tornado pulls it diagonally — Balloon's path curves into King Tower range. King fires 3 shots (336 damage). Princess tower fires 4 shots (448). Balloon takes 784 total damage. It reaches your tower with 856 HP remaining — one bomb (1,248) still drops, but the death bomb is prevented.

**Better Tornado:** Pull Balloon deeper into King Tower range. Place Tornado 1 tile from King, centered. Balloon now takes 6 King shots (672) + 5 Princess shots (560) = 1,232 damage. Balloon survives with 408 HP but drops zero bombs — it dies to tower before reaching. 3 elixir completely denies 5 elixir. +2 trade.

## Tier A Counters: Even or Slightly Positive

### Inferno Tower (5 elixir): The Beam

Inferno Tower ramps to 1,760 DPS. Balloon's 1,640 HP melts in 2.3 seconds (beam average: ~700 DPS over those 2.3 seconds). Placement: 4-3 center (4 tiles from river, 3 from princess tower). Balloon is pulled to the center, both towers shoot it, Inferno beam finishes it. Inferno Tower survives with 60% HP for the rest of the push (Lava Hound, support).

**Caution:** Freeze (4 elixir) on your Inferno Tower + tower. Balloon now reaches your tower with full HP. If you see Freeze in their deck, place Inferno Tower further back (5-3) so Freeze cannot cover both Inferno and princess tower simultaneously. They must choose which to Freeze.

### Tesla (4 elixir): Spell-Proof Defense

Tesla costs 4 elixir, deals 192 DPS, hides underground. It takes 11 hits (30 seconds) to kill Balloon alone — too slow. BUT: Tesla + two towers (112 + 112 = 224 DPS) + Tesla (192) = 416 combined DPS. Balloon dies in 3.9 seconds — before reaching the tower. Tesla costs 4 to counter 5. +1 trade.

Tesla's hiding mechanic makes it immune to spells. Earthquake does NOT damage hidden Tesla. Fireball, Poison, Arrows — all miss. This makes Tesla the safest building against spell-supported Balloon pushes.

## Tier B Counters: Slightly Negative but Accessible

### Mega Minion (3 elixir): The Flying Tank

Mega Minion: 219 DPS, 900 HP, flying. One Mega Minion takes 10 swings (15 seconds) to kill Balloon — way too slow. BUT: Mega Minion + two towers = 219 + 224 = 443 DPS. Balloon dies in 3.7 seconds. Mega Minion costs 3 to counter 5. +2 trade AND Mega Minion survives for counter-push.

**Placement:** 4 tiles from river, directly in Balloon's flight path. Mega Minion gets 3 swings before Balloon reaches the tower. Combined with tower damage: Balloon dead.

### Musketeer (4 elixir): The Ranged Sniper

Musketeer: 218 DPS, 6.0 range. Place her 5 tiles from the river, centered. She gets 5 shots (1,090 damage) before Balloon reaches the tower. Tower adds 3 shots (336). Total: 1,426. Balloon survives with 214 HP, drops one bomb (1,248 damage to your tower). This is a negative trade — you spent 4 elixir AND took 1,248 tower damage to counter their 5 elixir. Net: -1 elixir, -1,248 HP. Unacceptable.

**Fix:** Add Ice Spirit (1 elixir). Ice Spirit freezes Balloon for 1.5 seconds. Musketeer gets 1 extra shot (218). Total: 1,090 + 336 + 218 = 1,644 > 1,640. Balloon dies before reaching tower. Musketeer + Ice Spirit (5 elixir) counters Balloon (5 elixir) for an even trade. Your tower takes zero damage.

## Tier C Counters: Desperation or Specific Decks

### Minion Horde (5 elixir): High Risk, High Reward

Minion Horde: 6 Minions, 782 combined DPS. Kills Balloon in 2.1 seconds. 5 elixir vs 5 elixir — even trade. BUT: if they have Arrows (3 elixir), your Minion Horde dies instantly. Now: -5 elixir, no defense, Balloon at full HP heading to tower. You lose the tower. Only use Minion Horde against Balloon if you have baited their Arrows OR you know they do not run small spells.

### Archers (3 elixir): Budget Defense

Archers: 196 combined DPS (98 each). Two Archers + two towers: 196 + 224 = 420 DPS. Balloon dies in 3.9 seconds. Archers cost 3 for 5. +2 trade. BUT: Archers die to Arrows, Zap + tower, Fireball, Poison. If they support Balloon with any spell, your Archers die and Balloon connects. Use only against unsupported Balloon.

## Common Balloon Counter Mistakes

**Mistake 1: Placing all air defense in one spot.** If you stack Musketeer + Mega Minion + Tesla in one tile, one Freeze (4) disables all three. Spread them: Tesla at 4-3 center, Musketeer at 5-3, Mega Minion at 4-4 opposite.

**Mistake 2: Building placement too close to river.** Cannon at 3-3 (3 tiles from river) can be Freeze'd along with your tower. Place buildings at 4-3 or 5-3 — outside Freeze radius from tower.

**Mistake 3: Ignoring the death bomb.** Balloon's death bomb (798 area damage) kills wounded support troops. If you defended with low-HP troops near your tower, the death bomb kills them all. Pull Balloon AWAY from your tower (Tornado, building placement) before it dies.

**Mistake 4: Not activating King Tower.** If you have Tornado, activating King Tower on the first Balloon push wins the match. Every subsequent Balloon push takes 35% more damage. One 3-elixir activation generates 500+ value over the match. This is the single highest-value play against Balloon in Clash Royale.
