## Why P.E.K.K.A Dominates Ground — And Why She Is Easy to Counter

![PEKKA counter guide](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

P.E.K.K.A costs 7 elixir. At tournament standard (level 11): 3,760 HP, 678 damage per swing, 1.8-second attack speed (377 DPS). She is the single highest damage-per-swing troop in Clash Royale — one swing one-shots Musketeer, Wizard, Electro Wizard, Ice Wizard, and most 4-elixir support.

But P.E.K.K.A has three fatal weaknesses:
1. **Slow attack speed (1.8s):** Swarms bury her in bodies. 15 skeletons = 15 swings = 27 seconds. She dies long before.
2. **Targets ground only:** Air troops (Mega Minion, Minions, Bats, Baby Dragon) are completely immune. She stares at them, doing nothing.
3. **Slow movement:** Kite her across the arena with Ice Golem while your towers chip her down. 2 elixir beats 7.

RoyaleAPI data: P.E.K.K.A has a 48% win rate above 5,000 trophies — one of the lowest for a 7-elixir card. The reason: every competent player knows how to counter her.

## Tier S Counters: Positive Trades (Spend Less, Get More)

### Skeleton Army (3 elixir): The Hardest Counter

15 skeletons × 67 DPS = 1,005 combined DPS. P.E.K.K.A attacks once every 1.8 seconds, killing ONE skeleton per swing. To clear all 15: 15 swings × 1.8s = 27 seconds. In 27 seconds, skeletons deal 1,005 × 27 = 27,135 damage — enough to kill P.E.K.K.A (3,760 HP) in 3.7 seconds.

**Net trade:** 3 elixir for 7. +4 trade. Surviving skeletons (10+) counter-push.

**Counter-play:** Zap (2 elixir) kills 12 skeletons, leaving 3. P.E.K.K.A clears them in 5.4 seconds. Zap + P.E.K.K.A = 9 elixir vs your 3. You are still +6... wait, no. If they Zap your Skeleton Army, you lost 3 elixir, they spent 2, P.E.K.K.A survives. Bait their Zap before using Skeleton Army.

### Ice Golem (2 elixir): The Kite That Wins Matches

Place Ice Golem (2 elixir) at 4-4 center (toward the opposite lane from P.E.K.K.A). P.E.K.K.A turns and chases Ice Golem. Both towers shoot P.E.K.K.A. Ice Golem has 1,100 HP. P.E.K.K.A: 2 swings to kill (678 + 678 = 1,356 > 1,100). Ice Golem dies in 3.6 seconds. P.E.K.K.A has taken: 2 towers × 112 DPS × 3.6s = 806 damage. Ice Golem death slow adds 2 seconds of kiting. Total damage to P.E.K.K.A: 806 + 2 × 224 = 1,254.

P.E.K.K.A now at 3,760 - 1,254 = 2,506 HP and is in the center of the arena. Drop Skeletons (1 elixir) to kite her further. Total: 3 elixir for ~1,500 damage to P.E.K.K.A + she is nowhere near your tower. +4 trade.

### Guards (3 elixir): Shield + Spear

3 Guards with shields. Each has 244 HP shield + 108 HP body. P.E.K.K.A's 678-damage swing: shield absorbs full hit. Guard body takes zero damage. P.E.K.K.A needs 6 swings to kill all 3 Guards (2 swings each: 1 for shield, 1 for body). 6 × 1.8s = 10.8 seconds. Guards deal 3 × 108 = 324 DPS. Over 10.8 seconds: 3,499 damage. P.E.K.K.A dies at 3,760 HP... but towers are also shooting.

Combined: Guards (324 DPS) + 2 towers (224 DPS) = 548 DPS. P.E.K.K.A dies in 6.9 seconds. Guards survive with shields gone but bodies intact. 3 elixir for 7. +4 trade.

## Tier A: Even or Slightly Positive

### Inferno Tower (5 elixir): The Beam

Inferno Tower at 4-3 center pulls P.E.K.K.A. Beam ramps to 1,760 DPS. P.E.K.K.A's 3,760 HP: 3.0 seconds to kill (beam averages ~1,250 over those 3 seconds). 5 elixir for 7. +2 trade. Inferno Tower survives with 50% HP.

**Zap counter-play:** If they Zap your Inferno Tower, beam resets. But your Ice Spirit (1 elixir) absorbs the Zap: place Ice Spirit between P.E.K.K.A and Inferno Tower. They Zap the Ice Spirit. Inferno Tower locks on and kills P.E.K.K.A.

### Bats (2 elixir): Air Immunity, 335 DPS

5 Bats = 5 × 67 = 335 DPS. P.E.K.K.A cannot target air. Bats attack freely. P.E.K.K.A's 3,760 HP: dies in 11.2 seconds to Bats alone. + 2 towers (224 DPS): 559 DPS combined. Dies in 6.7 seconds. 2 elixir for 7. +5 trade. BUT: Bats die to Zap (one-shots all 5), Electro Wizard (spawn damage), Arrows. Bait small spells first.

### Mega Minion (3 elixir): Flying, Cannot Be Hit

Mega Minion: 219 DPS, flying. P.E.K.K.A ignores it entirely. Mega Minion + 2 towers = 443 DPS. P.E.K.K.A dies in 8.5 seconds. 3 elixir for 7. +4 trade. Mega Minion survives at full HP for counter-push.

## Tier B: Swarm + Distraction Combos

### Tombstone (3 elixir): Endless Skeletons

Tombstone spawns 1 skeleton every 2.9 seconds for 40 seconds — 14 skeletons total. P.E.K.K.A kills 1 skeleton per 1.8 seconds (her attack speed). Tombstone spawns 1 every 2.9 seconds. She kills faster than it spawns, so skeletons accumulate slower but never stop. Over 40 seconds, all 14 skeletons deal damage. Combined with 2 towers: P.E.K.K.A dies about 15 seconds in. 3 elixir for 7. +4 trade.

### Witch (5 elixir): Skeletons Every 5 Seconds

Witch spawns 3 skeletons every 5 seconds. P.E.K.K.A kills 1 per 1.8 seconds. In 5 seconds: 3 new skeletons spawn, P.E.K.K.A kills 2.7 skeletons. Net: skeletons accumulate by 0.3 per 5 seconds. It is slow, but over 60 seconds: 3.6 extra skeletons. Combined with Witch's own DPS (88) and towers (224): total ~350 DPS. P.E.K.K.A dies in ~10 seconds. 5 for 7. +2 trade. Witch survives for counter-push.

## P.E.K.K.A Bridge Spam: The Real Threat

P.E.K.K.A at the bridge with support (Bandit, Battle Ram, Dark Prince) is the real P.E.K.K.A archetype — Bridge Spam. P.E.K.K.A tanks while support swarms your tower. Countering P.E.K.K.A alone is not enough.

**Defense sequence against P.E.K.K.A Bridge Spam (15+ elixir):**
1. Inferno Tower at 4-3 center locks onto P.E.K.K.A
2. Ice Golem (2) kites support troops away from tower
3. Guards (3) or Skeletons (1) on Battle Ram
4. Mega Minion (3) on Bandit
5. Total: 5+2+3+3 = 13 elixir vs their 15+. P.E.K.K.A dies to Inferno. Support dies to your troops. Surviving troops counter-push.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1: Tanking P.E.K.K.A with a single heavy troop.** Knight (3, 1,988 HP): P.E.K.K.A kills him in 3 swings (5.4 seconds). Knight deals zero damage because he is dead. Tanking P.E.K.K.A with single troops is always negative. Use swarms or buildings.

**Mistake 2: Placing defense at the bridge.** P.E.K.K.A at bridge + support behind. If you place Skeleton Army at the bridge, support (Dark Prince, Wizard) splash-kills them instantly. Place Skeleton Army ON TOP of P.E.K.K.A, behind the bridge, where support splash cannot reach.

**Mistake 3: Ignoring P.E.K.K.A after she crosses the bridge.** She deals 678 per swing to your tower. Your tower has 2,534 HP. 4 swings = 2,712 = tower dead. She reaches the tower and kills it in 7.2 seconds. Never let P.E.K.K.A connect to your tower. Pull her with buildings or kite with Ice Golem.
