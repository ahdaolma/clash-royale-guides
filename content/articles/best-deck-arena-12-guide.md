## Arena 12 (Spooky Town): The Final Gate Before Legendary

![Spooky Town Arena 12](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Arena 12 (Spooky Town, 3600-4000 trophies) is the last arena before Legendary Arena. This is where players either solidify their fundamentals and break through to 4000+, or bounce between 3600-3900 for weeks. The unlocks: **Wall Breakers (2), Skeleton Dragons (4), Graveyard (5), Mother Witch (4), and Skeleton King (4-Champion).**

Wall Breakers at 2 elixir deal 440 area damage to buildings per pair — the highest damage-per-elixir tower chip in the game. Graveyard spawns 16 skeletons over 10 seconds for 1,072 total damage to an undefended tower. Combined with Freeze (unlocked in Arena 8), this is the Graveyard Freeze archetype that has won multiple global tournaments.

RoyaleAPI data shows Arena 12 has the steepest difficulty curve before 5000 trophies: win rates are nearly 50/50 across all decks because players here actually understand their archetypes. The difference is execution, not deck choice.

## Deck #1: Wall Breakers Miner Cycle (61% Win Rate)

![Wall Breakers cycle](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.abc123&f=1)

Wall Breakers are the cheapest win condition in Clash Royale at 2 elixir per pair. Each Wall Breaker deals 220 area damage to buildings. Two connect = 440 tower damage. In a cycle deck, you can play Wall Breakers every 10 seconds (2 elixir × 2.8 + cycle). Over 3 minutes: 18 connections. Even if only 40% connect (7 connections), that is 3,080 tower damage — more than a tower's 2,534 HP.

The deck pairs Wall Breakers with Miner to tank tower shots and provide a secondary damage source.

**Average elixir: 2.5** — even faster than 2.6 Hog Cycle.

### Miner + Wall Breakers Timing

The combo: Miner on tower (3 elixir), Wall Breakers at bridge (2 elixir). Miner arrives first, tanking tower shots. Wall Breakers arrive 1 second later. Tower shoots Miner — not Wall Breakers. Both Wall Breakers connect. 440 tower damage. Total cost: 5 elixir.

Repeat every 15 seconds. Five combos = 2,200 tower damage. Sixth combo = tower dead. The opponent MUST spend elixir to defend Miner, Wall Breakers, or both — and at 2.5 average elixir, you out-cycle every defensive answer.

### Magic Archer + Tornado: Geometry

Magic Archer's arrow penetrates through troops, dealing 132 damage to everything in a line. Tornado (3 elixir) pulls troops into a tight cluster. If their tower is behind the cluster, Magic Archer's arrow hits the tower AND every troop in the cluster. Total: 132 tower damage + 132 × number of pulled troops per arrow. If you pull 5 troops: 132 + 660 = 792 damage per arrow from a 4-elixir card. This chip adds up fast.

## Deck #2: Graveyard Freeze (60% Win Rate)

![Graveyard Freeze deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.abc123&f=1)

Graveyard (5 elixir) spawns 16 skeletons over 10 seconds around the target tower. Each skeleton deals 67 DPS. If 5 skeletons are hitting the tower simultaneously (very common), that is 335 DPS. Over 10 seconds: 3,350 potential damage. The tower kills skeletons at roughly 1 per second. Net damage: roughly 1,000-1,500 per Graveyard if partially defended, 2,000+ if unopposed.

Add Freeze (4 elixir, 4-second duration): you freeze their tower AND their defensive troops. For 4 seconds, skeletons spawn unimpeded. 4 seconds × 5 skeletons average = 20 skeleton-seconds × 67 DPS = 1,340 damage... plus the skeletons that accumulate during freeze continue attacking after it ends.

| Bomb Tower | 4 | Ground defense |
| Tornado | 3 | Pull / defense |
| Ice Spirit | 1 | Cycle |

**Average elixir: 3.4**

### Ice Golem + Graveyard + Freeze

The full combo: Ice Golem (2) at the bridge to tank, Graveyard (5) on the tower, Freeze (4) the tower + defense. Total: 11 elixir. Ice Golem tanks 3-4 tower shots (1,100 HP). Graveyard spawns skeletons for 10 seconds. Freeze locks the tower for 4 seconds.

Timing: Play Ice Golem first, wait until it crosses the bridge and draws tower aggro, THEN Graveyard, THEN Freeze 2 seconds later (after opponent has dropped their defense). If you Freeze immediately, you Freeze nothing — they have not dropped defense yet. The 2-second delay baits their defense first.

### When to Poison Instead of Freeze

Poison (4 elixir) covers the Graveyard area and deals 810 damage over 8 seconds. Against defensive Graveyard counters (Goblin Gang, Skeleton Army, Archers), Poison kills them AND chips the tower simultaneously. Use Freeze against building-heavy defense (Cannon, Tesla). Use Poison against swarm-heavy defense.

## Deck #3: Skeleton Dragons Lava Hound (58% Win Rate)

![Skeleton Dragons Lava Hound](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.abc123&f=1)

Skeleton Dragons cost 4 elixir for 2 flying units with 155 splash damage each. They are the budget alternative to Baby Dragon — slightly less HP (550 vs 1,074) but higher DPS (310 combined vs 133, over 2× the damage). Behind a Lava Hound (7 elixir), Skeleton Dragons deal massive splash to defending swarms.

### Skeleton Dragons vs Baby Dragon: The Math

Baby Dragon: 133 splash DPS, 1,074 HP, 4 elixir. Skeleton Dragons: 310 combined splash DPS, 550 HP each (1,100 total), 4 elixir. Against Minion Horde (5 elixir, 230 HP each): Baby Dragon takes 10.4 seconds to clear (133 DPS, but splash hits multiple — actually ~3 seconds to kill 6). Skeleton Dragons: 310 splash DPS, area hits 3-4 at once. Clear in 2 seconds. Skeleton Dragons are strictly better against air swarms.

Against ground support (Wizard, Musketeer): Baby Dragon tanks better (1,074 HP vs 550 per dragon). One Fireball kills both Skeleton Dragons (550 HP each < 305 Fireball + decay). Baby Dragon survives Fireball. Choose based on opponent's spells.

## Mother Witch: The Silent Game-Winner

Mother Witch costs 4 elixir. Her attack turns enemies into Cursed Hogs (318 damage, 484 HP each) when they die. Against Skeleton Army (15 skeletons): each skeleton killed becomes a Hog. 15 Hogs × 318 = 4,770 damage rushing at their tower. This is not an exaggeration — a single Mother Witch behind a tank against a swarm deck wins the game in one push.

However, Mother Witch has only 816 HP at level 9 — dies to Fireball (305) + Zap (192) = 497, two-shot by Musketeer. Protect her carefully. She is a support card, not a tank.
