## 10 Tips to Counter Every Balloon Push

### 1. Tornado to King Tower: The 3-Elixir Game-Winner

First Balloon push of the match: Tornado it into King Tower. King activates. Every future Balloon takes 3 towers × 112 DPS = 336 DPS instead of 2 towers × 112 = 224. That is 50% more damage. Over 4 Balloon pushes: ~500 extra damage to each Balloon = 2,000 total bonus. All for 3 elixir. This is the #1 Balloon counter in the game.

### 2. Tesla + Two Towers Math: 416 Combined DPS

Tesla (192 DPS) + both towers (224 DPS) = 416 DPS. Balloon HP: 1,640. Time to kill: 1,640 ÷ 416 = 3.94 seconds. Balloon takes 4.5 seconds to reach the tower from the bridge. It dies before reaching. Tesla costs 4. Balloon costs 5. +1 trade, tower safe, Tesla survives.

### 3. Do Not Cluster Your Air Defense

One Freeze (4 elixir) covers a 3-tile radius. If Musketeer + Mega Minion + Tesla are all in that radius, Freeze disables everything. Spread them: Tesla at 4-3 center, Musketeer at 5-3, Mega Minion at 4-4 opposite lane. At least one defense survives Freeze. Balloon still dies.

### 4. Rocket + Any Troop Hit = Balloon Dead

Rocket (1,232) + one Musketeer shot (218) = 1,450. Not enough (Balloon: 1,640). Two Musketeer shots (436): 1,232 + 436 = 1,668 > 1,640. Balloon dies. Time your Rocket so Musketeer gets 2 shots before Balloon reaches tower. Or: Rocket + Mega Minion 2 swings (438) = 1,670 > 1,640. Rocket is not a solo counter — pair it with any air-targeting troop.

### 5. Building Placement: The Freeze Gap

Freeze covers 3 tiles radius. Tower to 4-3 building placement: ~5 tiles apart. Freeze cannot cover both. Tower to 3-3 building: ~3.5 tiles apart. Freeze covers both. Always place defensive buildings at 4-3 or 5-3 to stay outside Freeze radius from your tower.

### 6. Inferno Tower: The 2.3-Second Kill

Inferno Tower placement: 4-3 center. Pulls Balloon. Beam ramps to 1,760 DPS. Balloon dies in 2.3 seconds. Surviving Inferno Tower (60%+ HP) now tanks for your tower against Lava Hound or support. BUT: if they Zap your Inferno at 2 seconds, beam resets. Have a backup — Ice Spirit or Skeletons to delay while beam ramps again.

### 7. Musketeer + Ice Spirit: The 5-for-5 Even Trade

Musketeer (4) gets 5 shots before Balloon reaches tower. Tower adds 3 shots. Total: 1,090 + 336 = 1,426. Balloon survives at 214 HP, drops one bomb. Add Ice Spirit (1): freeze adds 1 extra Musketeer shot (218). Total: 1,644 > 1,640. Balloon dead. Total cost: 5 elixir vs 5. Tower takes zero damage.

### 8. Minion Horde Kills in 2.1 Seconds — But Dies to Arrows

Minion Horde (5 elixir, 782 DPS) kills Balloon in 2.1 seconds. Even trade, fast kill. BUT: one Arrows (3) = -2 trade + Balloon at full HP + tower dead. Only use Minion Horde if: (a) you baited Arrows with another card, or (b) you tracked their cycle and Arrows is 4+ cards deep.

### 9. Death Bomb Kills Your Defense — Pull Balloon Away

Balloon's death bomb deals 798 area damage. Your Musketeer (656 HP) defending near the tower dies to the death bomb. Solution: use Tornado or building placement to pull Balloon AWAY from your tower and troops before it dies. If it dies over the river, death bomb hits nothing. If it dies over your tower, it kills your troops AND chips your tower.

### 10. Track Their Balloon Cycle

Balloon costs 5 elixir. Most Balloon decks have 3.5-4.0 average elixir. Cycle time: 4 cards × 3.5 avg = 14 elixir = 39 seconds. Always know: do they have Balloon in hand? If they just played it and you killed it, you have 39 seconds of safety. In those 39 seconds, push aggressively. They cannot counter-push with Balloon. This is how you turn Balloon defense into offensive momentum.
