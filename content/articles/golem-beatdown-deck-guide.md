## What Is Golem Beatdown?

![Clash Royale card](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse2.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.MBQiMthUgwJiWx7WqqEA1AHaD7&f=1)


Golem Beatdown is the heaviest archetype in Clash Royale. The core strategy: sacrifice tower damage in single elixir to build an unstoppable death ball in double elixir. A Golem push in overtime with Night Witch, Lumberjack, and a well-timed Lightning spell can take a tower from full HP in a single push.

The standard 2026 Golem deck at top ladder (Grand Champion and above):

- **Golem** (8 elixir) ? Win condition and primary tank
- **Night Witch** (4 elixir) ? Bat spawner, main support DPS
- **Lumberjack** (4 elixir) ? Rage on death, tank killer
- **Baby Dragon** (4 elixir) ? Splash support, air coverage
- **Tornado** (3 elixir) ? Control, King Tower activation
- **Barbarian Barrel** (2 elixir) ? Cheap ground control, cycle
- **Lightning** (6 elixir) ? Building killer, support troop eliminator
- **Bomber Evolution** (2 elixir) ? Ground splash, cycle card

Average elixir: 4.1. This is a heavy, expensive deck that demands patience.

## The Three Phases of Golem Beatdown

### Phase 1: Single Elixir ? Survive (0:00 - 2:00)

**Do not Golem in single elixir.** This is the number one mistake. If you Golem at 10 elixir in single, your opponent rushes the opposite lane and you have 2 elixir to defend. You lose your tower by 1:30.

Instead:
- Cycle Bomber Evolution, Barbarian Barrel, or Baby Dragon at the bridge to chip
- Use Tornado + Baby Dragon for defense
- Activate King Tower with Tornado if possible
- Defend with as little elixir as possible
- Take up to 1000 tower damage ? it is acceptable

Your goal in single elixir is to enter double elixir with your King Tower activated and your Crown Tower above 2000 HP.

### Phase 2: Double Elixir ? Build (2:00 - Overtime)

At exactly 2:00 (or when you hit 10 elixir after), place **Golem behind your King Tower** in the corner. This is the 3-0 plant (furthest corner from the river). This gives you maximum time to build elixir before Golem reaches the bridge.

As Golem walks:
1. Seven elixir regenerated by the time Golem crosses the river ? drop Night Witch behind Golem
2. Three elixir later ? Lumberjack behind Night Witch
3. Baby Dragon to cover air
4. Keep Lightning ready in hand

**When to Lightning:**
- Opponent places Inferno Tower ? Lightning immediately
- Opponent places Wizard/Executioner behind tower ? Lightning both tower and troop
- Opponent places Musketeer + Building ? Lightning both

Lightning deals 1086 damage to 3 targets at tournament standard. It one-shots Musketeer, Wizard, Electro Wizard, and Ice Wizard. It leaves Inferno Tower at 10% HP.

### Phase 3: Overtime ? Overwhelm

In overtime, elixir generates at double speed. Once your first Golem push is defended, immediately start building a second one. The opponent's counter cards will be out of cycle. This is when Golem Beatdown is strongest ? you can chain Golem pushes with only a 10-second gap between them.

## Matchup Guide

### Vs. Log Bait (Favorable)
Golem tanks Princess and Goblin Barrel chip. Lightning kills Inferno Tower. Tornado pulls everything into Baby Dragon splash. Play passively in single, push in double. Win rate: ~65%.

### Vs. Hog Cycle (Unfavorable)
Hog Rider out-cycles your Golem. They will spam Hogs in the opposite lane every time you Golem. Solution: only Golem when you have Barbarian Barrel + Bomber in cycle to defend cheaply. Win rate: ~40%.

### Vs. LavaLoon (Even)
Both decks want to build pushes. Whoever takes a tower first usually wins. Use Tornado to pull Lava Hound and Balloon away from your tower. Save Lightning for their support troops. Win rate: ~50%.

### Vs. X-Bow Siege (Favorable)
Golem in front of X-Bow blocks all damage. Lightning kills the X-Bow in 2 hits plus tower damage. Play Golem in the same lane as X-Bow. Win rate: ~70%.

### Vs. P.E.K.K.A Bridge Spam (Unfavorable)
P.E.K.K.A shreds Golem. Battle Ram punishes opposite lane. You need to out-cycle their P.E.K.K.A. Only push when P.E.K.K.A is out of cycle. Tornado to pull Battle Ram away from tower. Win rate: ~35%.

## Pro Tips

![Clash Royale card](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.49OnXTrnCaPWS5daiyb8gAAAAA&f=1)


1. **The 10-Second Rule:** After placing Golem, count to 10 before placing any support troops. This ensures you have maximum elixir for the push.

2. **Split Lane Pressure:** In double elixir, if you have an elixir lead, drop Golem in one lane and Bomber Evolution in the other. The opponent must split their defense.

3. **Golem Death Damage:** Golemites deal 259 death damage each (518 total). Against a tower under 500 HP, the Golemites alone can finish it. Do not overspend on support.

4. **Night Witch Placement:** Always place Night Witch 2 tiles behind Golem. If placed directly behind, spells like Fireball hit both. If placed too far, Bats do not reach the tower.

5. **Lumberjack Rage Timing:** The Lumberjack should die as the Golemites are popping. This way the Rage spell affects the Golemites AND the surviving support troops.

## Key Takeaways

Golem Beatdown is a test of patience. In single elixir, you are not playing to win ? you are playing to not lose. In double elixir, one well-executed push wins the game. Master the timing of your Lightning and the placement of your support troops, and Golem becomes one of the most reliable win conditions in the game.
