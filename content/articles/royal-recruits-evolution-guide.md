## Why Royal Recruits Evolution Dominates Split-Lane

![Royal Recruits Evolution](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Royal Recruits Evolution costs 7 elixir (2-cycle). 6 Recruits spawn — 3 in each lane. Each has a **244 HP shield** AND **charges after 2 tiles**, dealing double damage (276 per hit instead of 138). Total stats: 6 × (532 HP + 244 shield) = **4,656 effective HP.** Charge burst: 6 × 276 = 1,656 damage if all connect.

This is the highest total HP of any 7-elixir card. P.E.K.K.A has 3,760 HP for 7 elixir. Recruits Evo has 4,656 HP, split across 6 bodies with shields. They are a walking HP wall that applies pressure to both lanes simultaneously.

RoyaleAPI data (June 2026): Recruits Evo has a 54% win rate. Best in split-lane decks and Fireball Bait.

## Split-Lane Pressure: The Core Concept

3 Recruits go left, 3 go right. Opponent must defend both lanes. Total defense needed: at least 6 elixir for left lane + 6 for right = 12 elixir. Most opponents have 8-10. One lane connects.

**The math:** each unopposed Recruit deals 138 DPS (276 first hit). 3 Recruits on a tower: 414 DPS (828 first-hit burst). Tower (2,534 HP) dies in 6.1 seconds. Opponent MUST defend both lanes or lose a tower.

---

## Shield + Charge Interaction

Each shield absorbs one full hit. Against P.E.K.K.A (678/swing): shield breaks, Recruit takes zero body damage. P.E.K.K.A needs 2 swings per Recruit: 1 for shield, 1 for body. 6 Recruits = 12 swings = 21.6 seconds to clear all Recruits. In 21.6 seconds, 6 charging Recruits deal massive damage.

**Charge damage:** 276 first hit × 6 Recruits = 1,656 burst if all charge. Against a tower: 3 Recruits per lane × 276 = 828 burst per lane. Tower takes 828 damage instantly from the charge hits, then 414 DPS sustained.

## Defensive Recruits: The HP Wall

Recruits Evo placed defensively (behind your towers, splitting to both lanes) form a 4,656 HP wall. They absorb an entire beatdown push while your towers kill their tank. Against Golem + support: Recruits tank everything. Golem dies to towers. Recruits survive with 40% HP and counter-push down both lanes.

**Defensive value:** 7 elixir stops 16+ elixir of beatdown push. +9 effective trade. Then surviving Recruits become offensive pressure.
