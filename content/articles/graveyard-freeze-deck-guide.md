## Why Graveyard Freeze Has Been Meta for 9 Years

![Graveyard Freeze deck](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Ftse1.mm.bing.net%2Fth%2Fid%2FOIP.D9oHcRxA5aEAtPF_oFqfZgHaFW&f=1)

Graveyard costs 5 elixir and spawns 16 skeletons over 10 seconds around the target tower. Each skeleton deals 67 DPS at tournament standard. If 5 skeletons hit the tower simultaneously: 335 DPS × 10 seconds = 3,350 potential damage. Realistically, the tower kills ~1 skeleton per second, so net damage is roughly 1,000-1,500 per Graveyard. Add Freeze (4 elixir, 4-second tower/defense lock): an additional 4 seconds of unimpeded skeleton spawning adds 400-600 extra damage.

Graveyard Freeze has been tournament-viable since 2017 and has won multiple CRL finals. RoyaleAPI data (June 2026): 55% win rate at 5,000-7,000 trophies, 59% in Grand Challenges.

## The Exact Push Sequence

**0s:** Ice Golem (2 elixir) at the bridge. Opponent must defend or take 53 DPS to tower... which is negligible, but they do not know your hand yet.
**3s:** Ice Golem crosses bridge. Tower locks onto it.
**4s:** Graveyard (5 elixir) on their tower. Skeletons start spawning.
**5s:** Opponent reacts — drops Skeleton Army, Goblin Gang, Valkyrie, or Archers to defend.
**7s:** Freeze (4 elixir) the tower + their defensive troops. Everything frozen for 4 seconds.
**11s:** Freeze ends. Skeletons have been spawning for 7 seconds. Tower has taken 700-1,000 damage. Their defense is frozen and unable to act.
**14s:** Graveyard expires. Total damage: 900-1,500 tower HP.

Total elixir: 2+5+4 = 11 over 14 seconds (10 starting + 5 regenerated = 15 available). Opponent spent 3-5 elixir on defense that got frozen. Net: you have an elixir advantage AND tower damage.

## The 2-Second Freeze Delay: THE Most Important Trick

New Graveyard players Freeze immediately after playing Graveyard. This is wrong. If you Freeze at second 0 of Graveyard, you Freeze:
- The tower (good)
- Nothing else (bad)

The opponent has not dropped their defense yet. Your Freeze captures zero troops. The correct timing: **Freeze 2 seconds AFTER Graveyard.** This gives the opponent time to:
1. See the Graveyard
2. React by dropping their defense
3. Their defense lands and starts attacking skeletons
4. THEN you Freeze — trapping their defense AND the tower

The 2-second delay increases Freeze value from "4 seconds of tower lock" to "4 seconds of tower lock + 4 seconds of defense disabled + their 3-5 elixir wasted." It is the single most impactful timing improvement you can make.

## Ice Golem: The Unsung Hero

Ice Golem costs 2 elixir with 1,100 HP. Tower deals 112 DPS at level 11. Ice Golem tanks for 9.8 seconds (1,100 ÷ 112). Graveyard lasts 10 seconds. Almost perfect sync — Ice Golem tanks for roughly the entire Graveyard duration.

When Ice Golem dies, his death nova deals 84 damage and slows nearby enemies by 35% for 2 seconds. If their defensive Valkyrie is near Ice Golem when he dies, she is slowed — buying your skeletons 2 more seconds of damage.

**Ice Golem vs Knight (3 elixir) as tank:** Knight has 1,988 HP — tanks for 17.8 seconds. Overkill. You spend +1 elixir for tanking you do not need. Ice Golem is the optimal Graveyard tank.

## Poison vs Freeze: When to Use Which

Graveyard decks can run Poison (4 elixir) OR Freeze (4 elixir). The choice depends on the matchup:

| Factor | Freeze | Poison |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Against swarm defense | Freezes them, skeletons kill | Kills swarms over 8 seconds |
| Against building defense | Freezes building | Damages building + tower |
| Against tank defense (Valkyrie) | Freezes tank, skeletons hit tower | Valkyrie survives Poison, tanks skeletons |
| Surprise factor | High — 4s tower lock | Low — predictable area denial |
| Against Tornado | Tornado still works during Freeze | Tornado pulls skeletons into Poison |
| Against Mother Witch | Freeze her Hogs | Kills Mother Witch + Hogs over time |

**General rule:** Run Freeze if your opponent has swarm/building defense. Run Poison if they have Tornado or Mother Witch. At high ladder (6,000+), Poison is preferred because players predict Freeze. Below 6,000, Freeze has a higher win rate because opponents do not expect it.

## Tornado-Proof Graveyard Placement

Tornado (3 elixir) is Graveyard's hardest counter — it pulls all 16 skeletons into the center, away from the tower. Skeleton DPS drops to ~10% of normal.

**Counter-placement:** Do not center Graveyard on the tower. Offset Graveyard by 1 tile toward the OUTSIDE edge of the arena. Tornado pulls toward the center — if Graveyard is offset, some skeletons spawn outside Tornado's pull radius and continue hitting the tower. This reduces Tornado's effectiveness by ~30%.

## Upgrade Priority

1. **Graveyard** — skeleton level affects DPS (67 → 74 per level). +7 DPS × 5 skeletons = +35 DPS per level.
2. **Freeze** — duration increases by 0.3s per level. Level 11: 4.0s. Level 12: 4.3s. +0.3s = +2 extra skeleton hits.
3. **Ice Golem** — HP matters for tanking tower. Death slow duration increases.
4. **Bowler** — boulder damage breakpoints against Barbarians and Archers.
